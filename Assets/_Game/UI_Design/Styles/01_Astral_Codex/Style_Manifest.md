# Style Manifest: Astral Codex

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Ethereal, Mystical, Celestial.
**Colors**: Deep Indigo (#0A0E27), Cosmic Gold (#D4AF37), Astral Silver (#C0C0C0).
**Aesthetic**: Translucent glass panels with glowing runic borders. Stardust particle effects.
**Typography**: "Outfit" (Google Fonts) - sleek, modern, yet feels ancient.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Gold-rimmed glass rectangles. Hover triggers a faint pulsar glow.
- **Panels**: Semi-transparent indigo glass with blurred background (Frosted Glass).
- **Icons**: Thin-line gold icons with celestial symbols (moons, stars, constellations).

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: The chosen vassal is at the center, surrounded by a faint "Stardust" aura.
- **UI Layout**: Minimalist. Widgets float like constellations (Arknights style).
- **Navigation**: Orbs at the bottom for quick access to core modules.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: A shifting nebula. Level nodes are glowing stars.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen overlay for level details (no side windows).
- **Nodes**: Shimmering constellations that connect as you progress.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A portal opens into a deep space nebula.
- **Results Screen**: A 2x5 or 3x4 grid of holographic cards with starfield textures.
- **Reveal**: Cards flip over with a "supernova" transition effect.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Adaptive Grid Scroll View. Cards are organized by rarity constellations.
- **Filters**: Floating runic icons at the top for Element/Class filtering.
- **Stats**: Vertical Scroll View on the right for detailed attributes.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: A lattice of thin gold light on an indigo void.
- **HUD**: Corner-anchored constellations for unit portraits and skill energy.
- **VFX**: Damage numbers appear as bursts of starlight.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column Item Grid Scroll View.
- **Currency**: Glowing star shards and nebula essence at the top bar.

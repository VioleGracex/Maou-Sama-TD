# Style Manifest: Abyssal Forge

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Industrial, Dark Fantasy, Demonic Metallurgy.
**Colors**: Obsidian Black (#1A1A1A), Molten Orange (#FF4500), Iron Grey (#434343).
**Aesthetic**: Heavy metal plates, glowing cracks with lava flowing through. Mechanical steam and sparks.
**Typography**: "Titillium Web" - industrial, sharp edges, high contrast.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Riveted metal plates. Pressing triggers a "hissing steam" animation and glowing orange edges.
- **Panels**: Heavy obsidian slabs with internal orange glow from "cracks".
- **Icons**: Hammered iron symbols, chunky and solid.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: The vassal stands on a glowing forge platform. 
- **UI Layout**: Heavy, grounded. Buttons fixed to the bottom and side with "chains".
- **Interaction**: UI elements "slam" into place with industrial sound effects.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: A charred, volcanic landscape. Nodes are burning craters or iron fortresses.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen mission briefings with metal-plate borders.
- **Line Connectors**: Flows of molten lava.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A massive iron anvil is struck by a demonic hammer.
- **Results Screen**: A heavy iron-framed Grid Scroll View (2x5).
- **Reveal**: Characters emerge from cooling metal plates.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Industrial Vertical Scroll View with riveted dividers.
- **Filters**: Chunky iron toggle buttons for unit sorting.
- **Stats**: Displayed as "Pressure Gauges" or "Heat Levels" in a dedicated sub-panel.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Scratched metal plates with glowing orange seams.
- **HUD**: Rugged, iron-framed boxes. Health bars are glass tubes filled with lava.
- **VFX**: Steam, sparks, and tremors.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: Heavy iron grid (3x4) with items on stone pedestals.
- **Currency**: Soul Ingots and Demon Blood displayed in riveted gauges.

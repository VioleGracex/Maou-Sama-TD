# Citadel (Home) - Ethereal Silk Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Character Frame**: Framed by soft, drifting lilac silk banners and a slow rain of cherry blossoms.
- **Atmosphere**: Serene, soft-focus morning sunlight.

## Layout & Positioning (Standard Gacha Schema)

### 1. Top Navigation Bar
- **Anchor**: Top-Left / Top-Right
- **Buttons**:
    - `BtnBack` & `BtnHome`: Soft jade-trimmed buttons at top-left.
    - `Resource Counters`: Two hanging paper lanterns (Jade / Silk) at top-right.

### 2. Side Module Bar (Left Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Left
- **Stack**: 3 vertical hanging silk banners.
    - `BtnRanks`: Top.
    - `BtnDaily`: Middle.
    - `BtnChambers`: Lower-middle.
    - `BtnGrimore`: Bottom.

### 3. Main Dashboard Grid (Right Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Right
- **Grid Layout**:
    - **Row 1**: `CONQUEST` (Elegant horizontal silk scroll span).
    - **Row 2**: `COHORTS` and `VASSALS` — soft-focus vellum boxes.
    - **Row 3**: `MANDATES` and `TREASURY` (Stacked left) + `MANIFEST VASSALS` (Glowing lotus-floral block on right).

### 4. Edge Tabs (Far Right)
- **Anchor**: Right Center (Vertical)
- **Tabs**: `THRONE` and `VAULT` (Vertical paper tassels).

## Navigation & Interaction Logic
- **Module Entry**: "Ink-Wash" transition (Screen fades through a watercolor splash).
- **Sound**: Wind chimes, soft flute, and rustling paper.
- **Edit Mode**: Floating cherry blossoms guide the character to the parchment layout grid.

# Style Manifest: Ethereal Silk

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Elegant, Flowing, Zen-like.
**Colors**: Pearl White (#F8F8FF), Soft Lavender (#E6E6FA), Pale Jade (#98FB98).
**Aesthetic**: Flowing fabric animations, ink-wash textures, and soft-focus backgrounds.
**Typography**: "Cormorant Garamond" - sophisticated, serif, delicate.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Soft ribbons or brush strokes. Hovering causes a "ripple" effect across the text.
- **Panels**: Transparent layers resembling layered silk or vellum.
- **Icons**: Hand-drawn ink-wash symbols, very thin and minimalist.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: Framed by floating silk ribbons and falling cherry blossoms.
- **UI Layout**: Airy and spacious. Floating menus with slow drift.
- **Interaction**: Soft wind chimes and fabric rustling sounds.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: An ink-wash mountain landscape on a horizontal scroll.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen "Scroll Expansion" for level details.
- **Nodes**: Calligraphy stamps or blooming flowers.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A brush paints the character into existence on a giant scroll.
- **Results Screen**: A minimalist grid (2x5) on a textured paper background.
- **Reveal**: Cards flip over with an ink-splash transition.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Clean Grid Scroll View with plenty of vertical whitespace.
- **Filters**: Delicate brush-stroke icons at the top of the list.
- **Stats**: Vertical text layout with soft color washes.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Faint ink-wash lines on a textured paper surface.
- **HUD**: Minimalist, semi-transparent. Text-based status indicators.
- **VFX**: Petals, ink splashes, and soft light rays.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column Grid Scroll View on silk-textured panels.
- **Currency**: Jade beads and ink droplets displayed at the top corner.

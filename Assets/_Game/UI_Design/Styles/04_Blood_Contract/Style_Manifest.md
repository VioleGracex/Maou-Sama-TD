# Style Manifest: Blood Contract

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Gothic, Vampiric, Crimson Ritual.
**Colors**: Velvet Red (#8B0000), Slate Black (#2F4F4F), Silver Leaf (#C0C0C0).
**Aesthetic**: Dripping liquid textures (blood), silver filigree, and cracked marble.
**Typography**: "Cinzel" - architectural, stone-etched, authoritative.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Silver filigree frames. Clicking causes a red "bleed" effect from the center.
- **Panels**: Dark stone/marble slabs with silver ivy or filigree.
- **Icons**: Ritualistic daggers, bats, and chalices.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: Behind the character is a massive Gothic window with blood-red moonlight.
- **UI Layout**: Hierarchical, structured. Deep shadows and high contrast.
- **Interaction**: Clicks sound like sharp metal (daggers) or heavy stone doors.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: An ancient, blood-stained map of a dark empire.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen "Tombstone" overlays for mission data.
- **Nodes**: Gothic cathedrals or tomb-like structures.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A silver chalice overflows with blood, forming the character.
- **Results Screen**: 2x5 Grid of stone-textured cards on a velvet background.
- **Reveal**: Cards shatter or "bleed" into the character reveal.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Portrait Grid Scroll View with elaborate silver frames for each card.
- **Filters**: Candelabra icons or ritual symbols for sorting.
- **Stats**: Displayed on silver plaque-like boxes in a side-panel view.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Etched stone tiles with blood filling the cracks as units move.
- **HUD**: Elaborate silver frames. Health bars are vials of red liquid.
- **VFX**: Blood splatters, shadow manipulation, and red lightning.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column Grid on dark velvet-lined panels.
- **Currency**: Crimson teardrops and silver coins at the top HUD.

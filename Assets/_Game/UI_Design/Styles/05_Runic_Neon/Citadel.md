# Citadel (Home) - Runic Neon Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Character Frame**: High-tech runic scan-circle beneath the vassal.
- **Atmosphere**: Vibrant neon cyan and purple, "static" glitch effects.

## Layout & Positioning (Standard Gacha Schema)

### 1. Top Navigation Bar
- **Anchor**: Top-Left / Top-Right
- **Buttons**:
    - `BtnBack` & `BtnHome`: Flickering digital chevrons at top-left.
    - `Resource Counters`: Two digital data-stream monitors (Credits/Fragments) at top-right.

### 2. Side Module Bar (Left Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Left
- **Stack**: 3 Vertical AR-style window panes.
    - `BtnRanks`: Top.
    - `BtnDaily`: Middle.
    - `BtnChambers`: Lower-middle.
    - `BtnGrimore`: Bottom.

### 3. Main Dashboard Grid (Right Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Right
- **Grid Layout**:
    - **Row 1**: `CONQUEST` (Massive scan-line grid portal).
    - **Row 2**: `COHORTS` and `VASSALS` — cyan wireframe blocks.
    - **Row 3**: `MANDATES` and `TREASURY` (Stacked left) + `MANIFEST VASSALS` (Bright Magenta neon block on right).

### 4. Edge Tabs (Far Right)
- **Anchor**: Right Center (Vertical)
- **Tabs**: `THRONE` and `VAULT` (Flickering vertical light-bars).

## Navigation & Interaction Logic
- **Module Entry**: "Digitize" transition (Screen pixelates/glitches into the next view).
- **Sound**: High-frequency data-stream beeps and CRT monitor hums.
- **Edit Mode**: Floating AR-grid appears behind the character for precise alignment.

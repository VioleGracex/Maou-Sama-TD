# Style Manifest: Runic Neon

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Magipunk, Tech-Fantasy, Electric Sorcery.
**Colors**: Electric Cyan (#00FFFF), Void Purple (#4B0082), Laser Magenta (#FF00FF).
**Aesthetic**: High-tech digital interfaces combined with ancient glowing runes. Static and glitch effects.
**Typography**: "Rajdhani" - geometric, technical, high-speed.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Floating "holographic" prisms. Interaction causes a "pulse" of light that moves along runic lines.
- **Panels**: Glitch-textured digital fields with scrolling runic code.
- **Icons**: Circuitry lines that form arcane symbols.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: The vassal stands inside a floating runic scan-circle.
- **UI Layout**: Asymmetric, "AR" style overlay. Data streams on the peripherals.
- **Navigation**: Rapid "Terminal" transitions between pages.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: A digital wireframe of the world with glowing terrain.
- **Navigation**: Holographic pop-up displays for mission start.
- **Nodes**: Floating data clusters or magi-terminals.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A digital breakdown and re-assembly of data particles.
- **Results Screen**: A high-speed digital Grid Scroll View (2x5).
- **Reveal**: Character "materializes" through a glitch-portal.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Sleek Grid Scroll View with digital "Scan" lines moving over cards.
- **Filters**: Toggle switches with neon glow for sorting criteria.
- **Stats**: Animated histograms and radar charts with neon gradients.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: A glowing cyan laser grid on a dark floor.
- **HUD**: Futuristic "Pilot" style HUD. Real-time logging of combat data.
- **VFX**: Digital cubes, laser beams, and "hex" patterns.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column holographic Grid Scroll View.
- **Currency**: Crypto-runes and bio-static data in neon readouts.

# Citadel (Home) - Divine Marble Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Character Frame**: Vassal stands on a sunlit marble balcony with soft golden "God rays".
- **Atmosphere**: Pure, bright, with white feathers falling slowly.

## Layout & Positioning (Standard Gacha Schema)

### 1. Top Navigation Bar
- **Anchor**: Top-Left / Top-Right
- **Buttons**:
    - `BtnBack` & `BtnHome`: Polished ivory tiles with gold borders.
    - `Resource Counters`: Two golden chalices (Divine Favor / Gold) at top-right.

### 2. Side Module Bar (Left Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Left
- **Stack**: 3 vertical marble columns.
    - `BtnRanks`: Top.
    - `BtnDaily`: Middle.
    - `BtnChambers`: Lower-middle.
    - `BtnGrimore`: Bottom.

### 3. Main Dashboard Grid (Right Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Right
- **Grid Layout**:
    - **Row 1**: `CONQUEST` (Majestic marble frieze span).
    - **Row 2**: `COHORTS` and `VASSALS` — beveled ivory stone blocks.
    - **Row 3**: `MANDATES` and `TREASURY` (Stacked left) + `MANIFEST VASSALS` (Glowing sun-temple block on right).

### 4. Edge Tabs (Far Right)
- **Anchor**: Right Center (Vertical)
- **Tabs**: `THRONE` and `VAULT` (Vertical golden wings).

## Navigation & Interaction Logic
- **Module Entry**: "Flash of Light" transition (Screen whitens out and fades in).
- **Sound**: Angelic choir chords and the ringing of golden bells.
- **Edit Mode**: Golden rays of light emerge from the marble floor to show alignment grids.

# Conquest (Campaign Map) - Divine Marble Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Map Background**: A massive world relief carving on a white marble wall, detailed with gold leaf.
- **Nodes**: Glowing golden temples or white marble statues.
- **Connectors**: Threads of pure white light.

## Buttons & Navigation
| Button Name | Label / Icon | Redirects To | Visual Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `BtnBack` | Back Arrow | Citadel (Home) | A golden wing pointing left. |
| `BtnDiffToggle` | Difficulty | N/A (Internal) | A golden sun that brighten or dims based on setting. |
| `BtnStageNode` | Stage Number | Tactical Prep | A sun-symbol node that glows intensely. |
| `BtnRegionRewards`| Chest Icon | Rewards Popup | A golden amphora or decorative jar. |

## Internal Windows & Overlays
- **Stage Detail Overlay**: A white marble slab that rises from the floor with golden engravings.
- **Difficulty Popup**: Three golden banners (Mortal, Saint, God) that unfurl.
- **Tactical Prep Window**: A divine arena view with golden unit slots.

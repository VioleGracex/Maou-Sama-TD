# Manifest (Summon Altar) - Divine Marble Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Background**: A sunlit chamber within a mountain temple, with white feathers drifting slowly through beams of light.
- **Summon Altar**: A pure white marble basin that overflows with liquid light during the summon.

## Buttons & Navigation
| Button Name | Label / Icon | Redirects To | Visual Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `BtnBack` | Back Arrow | Citadel (Home) | Golden wing icon. |
| `BtnPull1` | Summon x1 | Summon Sequence| A glowing golden laurel. |
| `BtnPull10` | Summon x10 | Summon Sequence| A golden solar crown icon. |
| `BtnDetails` | Information | Details Window | Soft, white marble scroll icon. |
| `BtnBannerNext` | Right Arrow | Next Banner | A soft burst of divine light. |

## Internal Windows & Overlays
- **Summon Result Grid**: 2x5 Grid of ivory cards with gold-leaf borders. High rarity causes solar flared edges.
- **Character Reveal**: A pillar of light descends from the heavens, and the character steps out with a soft choir sound.
- **Pity Tracker**: A golden sun dial that fills with light as pulls are made.

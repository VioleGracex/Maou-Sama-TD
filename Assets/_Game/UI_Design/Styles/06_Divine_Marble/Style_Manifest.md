# Style Manifest: Divine Marble

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Majestic, Sacred, Greco-Roman Pantheon.
**Colors**: Ivory White (#FFFFF0), Royal Gold (#FFD700), Sky Blue (#87CEEB).
**Aesthetic**: Polished marble, golden laurel motifs, and sunbeams. Pure, clean, and bright.
**Typography**: "Playfair Display" - elegant, serif, feels like carved stone.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Beveled ivory tiles with gold inlaid text. Soft "harp" sound when clicked.
- **Panels**: Marble slabs with thin gold veins and ornate cornices.
- **Icons**: Golden sculptures or reliefs (scales, swords, crowns).

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: The vassal stands on a sunlit marble balcony.
- **UI Layout**: Balanced, symmetrical. Centered menus with golden dividers.
- **Interaction**: UI elements fade in with a soft, warm light.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: Bas-relief of the continent on a marble wall.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen "Sanctuary" view for stage selection.
- **Nodes**: Golden temples or statues that glow when unlocked.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: Bright light descends onto a marble altar.
- **Results Screen**: Regal 2x5 Grid on an ivory background with gold trim.
- **Reveal**: Character emerges from a pillar of divine light.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Clean Grid Scroll View with white marble frames for character portraits.
- **Filters**: Golden wing icons for sorting by class/type.
- **Stats**: High-contrast typography on beveled ivory side-panels.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Polished white stone squares with golden grout.
- **HUD**: Symmetrical golden frames. Health bars are bars of pure light.
- **VFX**: Golden sparks, light beams, and feathers.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column Grid with items on golden pedestals.
- **Currency**: Gold drachmas and divine ambrosia at the top HUD.

# Style Manifest: Monochrome Shade

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Noir, Sketch-like, Brutalist.
**Colors**: Pure Black (#000000), Stark White (#FFFFFF), Mid-Grey (#808080).
**Aesthetic**: Pencil sketches, charcoal textures, and high-contrast shadows. Minimalist but expressive.
**Typography**: "Baskerville" - clean, high-contrast serif.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Hand-drawn rectangles or circles. Thick, rough sketchy strokes.
- **Panels**: Stark white or black blocks with sketchy shading boundaries.
- **Icons**: High-contrast silhouettes or simple line drawings.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: Character displayed as a high-contrast charcoal sketch.
- **UI Layout**: Experimental, off-kilter text placement over sketch backgrounds.
- **Interaction**: UI elements "pop" in with sharp sketch transitions.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: Hand-drawn architectural blueprint or rough survey map.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen "Folder" transition for stage details.
- **Nodes**: Ink blots or simple X marks.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: Ink splashes on a white canvas forming the character.
- **Results Screen**: Sketchy Grid Scroll View (2x5) on a paper texture.
- **Reveal**: Character "unfolds" from a crumpled sketch.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Adaptive Grid of high-contrast thumbnail sketches.
- **Filters**: Simple hand-written text labels for sorting.
- **Stats**: Vertical bar charts using cross-hatch shading styles.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Rough pencil-drawn grid on a textured paper background.
- **HUD**: Minimalist white boxes. Health bars are simple black fill-ins.
- **VFX**: Pencil scribbles, ink splats, and flash-bulb effects.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column grid of line drawings on a ledger-style panel.
- **Currency**: Ink droplets and torn paper slips at the top.

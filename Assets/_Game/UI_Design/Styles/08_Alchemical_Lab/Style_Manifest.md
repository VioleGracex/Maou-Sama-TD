# Style Manifest: Alchemical Laboratory

## Core Visual Prompt
**Theme**: Scholar, Arcane Science, Steampunk Apothecary.
**Colors**: Parchment Tan (#F5F5DC), Copper (#B87333), Emerald Fluid (#50C878).
**Aesthetic**: Old parchment, copper pipes, bubbling liquids, and magnifying glasses. 
**Typography**: "EB Garamond" - academic, refined, old print.

## Component Design
- **Buttons**: Copper frames with small glass windows showing bubbling liquid.
- **Panels**: Stacked sheets of stained parchment held by copper clips.
- **Icons**: Lab equipment - flasks, gears, botanical drawings.

## Page Flows (Gacha Optimized)

### Citadel (Home)
- **Character Frame**: The character is viewed "through" a large circular lens.
- **UI Layout**: Cluttered with books, vials, and blueprints at the edges.
- **Interaction**: Sounds of glass clinking and liquid bubbling.

### Conquest (Campaign Map)
- **Map Style**: Detailed, hand-drawn map on yellowed parchment.
- **Navigation**: Full-screen "Research Log" view for mission start.
- **Nodes**: Wax seals or small alchemical symbols.

### Manifest (Summon Altar)
- **Summon Sequence**: A large boiling cauldron releases green vapor.
- **Results Screen**: 2x5 Grid on a stained parchment panel.
- **Reveal**: Character "condenses" from floating symbols.

### Vassals (Gallery)
- **Layout**: Grid Scroll View on a "Registry" parchment background.
- **Filters**: Ink-stamp icons for Unit Classification.
- **Stats**: Displayed in handwritten botanical-style sidebars.

### Battle (Tactical Grid)
- **Grid**: Layout of copper tubes and glass tiles.
- **HUD**: Magnifying lenses over icons. Health is shown in test tubes.
- **VFX**: Green smoke, sparks, and rotating gears.

### Treasury (Shop)
- **Display**: 3-column Grid with items labeled as "Specimens".
- **Currency**: Copper gears and philosopher's stones at the top bar.

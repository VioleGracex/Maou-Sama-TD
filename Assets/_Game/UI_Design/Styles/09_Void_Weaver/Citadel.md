# Citadel (Home) - Void Weaver Style

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Character Frame**: Vassal stands within a shifting fractal field.
- **Atmosphere**: Cosmic horror; pulsing purple energies and floating eyes.

## Layout & Positioning (Standard Gacha Schema)

### 1. Top Navigation Bar
- **Anchor**: Top-Left / Top-Right
- **Buttons**:
    - `BtnBack` & `BtnHome`: Shadowy chevrons with pulsing purple pupils at top-left.
    - `Resource Counters`: Two void orbs (Souls / Fractals) at top-right.

### 2. Side Module Bar (Left Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Left
- **Stack**: 3 vertical tears in reality.
    - `BtnRanks`: Top.
    - `BtnDaily`: Middle.
    - `BtnChambers`: Lower-middle.
    - `BtnGrimore`: Bottom.

### 3. Main Dashboard Grid (Right Alignment)
- **Anchor**: Middle-Right
- **Grid Layout**:
    - **Row 1**: `CONQUEST` (Wide shadow-tendril rift span).
    - **Row 2**: `COHORTS` and `VASSALS` — pulsing purple fractal blocks.
    - **Row 3**: `MANDATES` and `TREASURY` (Stacked left) + `MANIFEST VASSALS` (Giant central eye/black-hole block on right).

### 4. Edge Tabs (Far Right)
- **Anchor**: Right Center (Vertical)
- **Tabs**: `THRONE` and `VAULT` (Floating purple claw-tabs).

## Navigation & Interaction Logic
- **Module Entry**: "Abyss Sink" transition (Screen turns inside-out into a black hole).
- **Sound**: Eldritch whispers, low drones, and heartbeats.
- **Edit Mode**: Tentacles reach from the UI borders to hold the character in place on the grid.

# Maou-Sama TD: Game Design Document (V2.4)

## 1. Game Overview
**Title:** Maou-Sama TD (Working Title)
**Genre:** Tower Defense / RPG / Visual Novel
**Platform:** Mobile (Android/iOS) / PC
**Target Audience:** Fans of strategy games, anime aesthetics, and "Villain Protagonist" tropes.

### Core Concept
You are the **13th Demon Lord (Maou)**. Resurrected by a **Pact with the World** to settle a 50-year-old vendetta with the **Hero**.
But the World lied.
The Pact wasn't for a rematch. It was a draft. When the **Inverse World** invades in Act 3, you are forced by the Pact to *protect* your hated rival against the **First Maou**, the True Demon who seeks to reset all existence.

## 2. Gameplay Mechanics

### A. Tower Defense (The Battlefield)
*   **Perspective:** Isometric 2.5D or Top-Down.
*   **The Grid:** Battles exist on a grid-based map.
*   **Unit Placement:** Players deploy "Vassals" (Units) onto tiles.
*   **Cost Management:** Units cost "Authority" to deploy.
*   **Sovereign Power:** The Player (Maou) can intervene directly with global spells using **Authority Seals**.

### B. RPG Progression (The Citadel)
*   **Avatar Creation:** Choose Male or Female Maou.
*   **Gacha System:** Summon new Vassals using the **Summoning Ritual**.
*   **Unit Growth:** Level Up, Awakening (Stars), and Equipment.
*   **Affection System:** Give gifts to Vassals.
    *   **Ignis Special:** Unlocks "Memory Fragments" revealing her past as a Human Hero.

## 3. Game Modes

### A. Main Campaign
The core story, divided into **Acts**.
*   **Act 1:** The Awakening & The Betrayed Knight (Ignis).
*   **Act 2:** The Crusade & The Rival's Return.
*   **Act 3:** The Betrayal (Inverse World Invasion).

### B. Resource Dungeons
*   **Gold Vault:** High currency rewards.
*   **Exp Forest:** Experience materials.
*   **Material Mine:** Crafting/Ascension materials.

## 4. Characters & Classes

### Unit Classes (The Bestiary)

| Class | Icon | Role | Tactical Summary |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bastion** | 🛡️ | **Heavy Tank** | High HP/DEF. Block Count: 3-4. Essential for holding chokepoints. |
| **Vanguard** | ⚔️ | **Melee DPS** | Balanced stats. Block Count: 2. High SP regeneration while engaged. (e.g., Ignis) |
| **Executioner** | 🗡️ | **Burst Assassin** | High ATK/ASPD, low HP. Best for single-target elite elimination. |
| **Ranger** | 🏹 | **Physical Ranged** | High Ground deployment. Prioritizes Flying enemies. High range. (e.g., Shade) |
| **Warlock** | 🔮 | **Magical Ranged** | High Ground. AoE magic damage. Often applies CC (Slow/Stun). (e.g., Lilith) |
| **Sage** | ⚕️ | **Healer** | Restores HP to allies within range. Essential for Bastion longevity. |
| **Architect** | 🏗️ | **Fortifier** | Deploys stationary Towers or non-blocking Traps. Immune to standard melee. |
| **Necromancer** | 💀 | **Summoner** | Spawns temporary "Fodder" units to increase effective Block Count. |
| **Support** | 📣 | **Buffer** | Provides passive auras (ATK/DEF/SP speed) to nearby allies. |
| **Gunner** | 🔫 | **Rapid Fire** | Extreme ASPD. Deals True Damage but often consumes Authority per shot. |
| **Assassin** | 👤 | **Infiltrator** | Can be placed near enemy spawns. Ignores 1 enemy to strike the backline. |

### The Maou (Player)
The 13th incarnation. Manipulated by the World. Fueled by rage against the System that denies them their revenge.

### The Antagonists
1.  **The Church:** Corrupt human institution.
2.  **The Rival (Hero):** Your destined enemy.
3.  **The First Maou:** The creator of the Inverse World. The true threat.

## 5. Themes
*   **Betrayal:** Everyone betrays everyone (Church betrays Ignis, World betrays Maou).
*   **Forced Alliance:** Working with your enemy to survive.
*   **The Greater Evil:** The First Maou makes you look like a saint.

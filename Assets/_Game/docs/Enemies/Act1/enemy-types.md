# Act 1: Tomb of True Demons - Enemy Bestiary

## Overview
This document defines the enemies found in the Tomb of True Demons (Act 1). These creatures are born from the lingering resentments of dead demon lords and the stagnant mana of the tomb.

---

## 1. Lesser Shadow (Weak)
*   **Class**: Grunt / Swarmer
*   **Description**: Small, indistinct blobs of shadow that quickly scuttle across the floor. They have glowing white eyes but no other distinct features. They are weak individually but dangerous in large numbers, often overwhelming targets by sheer weight of hatred.
*   **Behavior**: Moves fast, attacks in groups. Low HP, Low Damage.

## 2. Shadow Construct (Weak/Medium)
*   **Class**: Warrior
*   **Description**: A humanoid shape roughly formed from coalesced shadows. It mimics the form of a soldier but lacks detail—limbs may end in spikes instead of hands. It is unstable, constantly shifting and dripping shadow-matter.
*   **Behavior**: Balance of offense and defense. Average speed.

## 3. Tomb Guardian (Medium/Tank)
*   **Class**: Tank
*   **Description**: An ancient suit of armor or a stone statue that has been possessed by shadow energy. It is heavy, slow, and durable. Purple energy leaks from the visor and joints of the armor.
*   **Behavior**: High HP, Slow movement, High physical resistance.

## 4. Ancient Spirit (Ranged/Support)
*   **Class**: Ranged / Debuffer
*   **Description**: A wisp-like entity, the tormented soul of a long-dead cultist or minor demon. It floats above the ground, wrapped in tattered ethereal robes. It attacks with bolts of cold, necrotic energy.

## Boss: Shadow of True Demon
*   **Class**: Boss
*   **Description**: A corrupted, incomplete resurrection of a forgotten demon lord. It is a roiling mass of black mana and bone, constantly shifting forms as if trying to remember who it once was. It radiates a aura of ancient, stagnant malice that chills the air.
*   **Behavior**: High Health, Area of Effect attacks, Summons Lesser Shadows. Uses "Failed Authority" to debuff player units.

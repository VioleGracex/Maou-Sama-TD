# Maou Tower Defense: Vassals of Aethelgard — Legendary Male Allies & Mythic Vassals
**10 Characters (All Male, Legendary, or Mythical, including two mythic "genderless" familiars for world depth)**

---

## 71. Ragnar, Berserker Chieftain
- **Race/Class**: Shifter (Ursine) / Bastion
- **Combat Wear**: Massive bearhide cloak, bead-encrusted leather armor, runic tattoos on chest and arms.
- **Weapon**: Spiked war club, steel jaw mask.
- **Loves**: Mead, clan feasts, a fair brawl.
- **Hates**: Weak-heartedness, broken pacts.
- **Lore**: Oldest and strongest of the Frostbound Clans, legendary for breaking the lines of the Holy Empire’s best formations.
- **Atmosphere**: Wild, raucous, awe-inspiring.
- **Personality**: Boisterous, humorous, fiercely protective of kin and the Maou.

---
## 72. Sigurd, The Oath-Keeper
- **Race/Class**: Human Betrayer / Vanguard
- **Combat Wear**: Knight’s regalia, heavy thunder-engraved vambraces, torn blue tabard.
- **Weapon**: Oathblade longsword, shield with faded family crest.
- **Loves**: Songs of battle, cold mornings, honest rivals.
- **Hates**: Treachery, forgotten promises.
- **Lore**: Once a Sanctus Commander, now Maou’s personal oathsworn champion.
- **Atmosphere**: Noble, tragic, relentless.
- **Personality**: Gallant, rigidly loyal, self-sacrificing.

---
## 73. Bale, Ironclad Juggernaut
- **Race/Class**: Construct (Mech) / Bastion
- **Combat Wear**: Towering iron frame, obsidian plating, infernal core glows within chest.
- **Weapon**: Transforming blade-cannon, steam fists.
- **Loves**: Logic puzzles, engineering duels, testing limits.
- **Hates**: Heat dissipation problems, rust.
- **Lore**: Built as a weapon of last resort by the Maou’s artificers; destroyed whole sanctus divisions before entering slumber.
- **Atmosphere**: Imposingly mechanical, uncanny.
- **Personality**: Strict, literal-minded, learning humor.

---
## 74. Nero, The Shadow King
- **Race/Class**: Undead (Wraith Lord) / Assassin
- **Combat Wear**: Tattered black cloak, golden mask, embroidered shadowweave under-armor.
- **Weapon**: Twin daggers, ethereal shadow whip.
- **Loves**: Night, silence, loyalty.
- **Hates**: Sun, broken secrets.
- **Lore**: Once a hero of old; betrayed, risen anew as wraith king—now the Maou’s spymaster.
- **Atmosphere**: Whispery, chilling, omnipresent.
- **Personality**: Softly spoken, incisive, absolute in discretion.

---
## 75. Dragan, The Storm Tyrant
- **Race/Class**: Mythological Hybrid (Dragonoid) / Warlock
- **Combat Wear**: Jeweled scale armor, flaming crown, lightning-wrapped tail.
- **Weapon**: Thunder staff, breath of the storm.
- **Loves**: Thunderstorms, flying at dusk.
- **Hates**: Weak magic, captivity.
- **Lore**: Claimed the skies and later pledged to Maou for a rematch in the afterlife.
- **Atmosphere**: Crackling, energetic, mesmerizing.
- **Personality**: Proud, tempestuous, surprisingly playful.

---
## 76. Gaius, The Golden Builder
- **Race/Class**: Construct (Architect Golem) / Tactician
- **Combat Wear**: Gold-plated alloy frame, filigreed cape, toolbelt of ancient design.
- **Weapon**: Geomantic rod, spirit blueprints.
- **Loves**: Puzzles, fortress design, teamwork.
- **Hates**: Disorderly construction, mud.
- **Lore**: Designed most of Maou’s citadels and traps; portions of his mind run every defense AI.
- **Atmosphere**: Towering, radiant.
- **Personality**: Patient, methodical, gentle with the young.

---
## 77. Vail, The Twilight Bard
- **Race/Class**: Human Betrayer / Support (Bard)
- **Combat Wear**: Shadow-dyed cloak, silver thread hat, inlaid magical strings along his gloves.
- **Weapon**: Whispering lyre, dazzling knives.
- **Loves**: Poetry, midnight balls, redemption arcs.
- **Hates**: Dull crowds, sanctus censors.
- **Lore**: Banished for subversive songs; his melodies now rally the Maou’s armies.
- **Atmosphere**: Melancholic, yet uplifting.
- **Personality**: Charismatic, moody, with a weakness for beauty.

## 78. Ulrich, Eternal Watcher
- **Race/Class**: The Fallen (Angel) / Bastion
- **Combat Wear**: Massive silver armor, white-gold tattered wings, emerald-studded crown.
- **Weapon**: Heavenly bulwark shield, fordgehammer.
- **Loves**: Vigilance, discipline, sunrise prayers.
- **Hates**: Lax guards, hypocrisy.
- **Lore**: Cast from the Luminary’s grace for pursuing forbidden loyalties.
- **Atmosphere**: Towering, inspirational.
- **Personality**: Ever alert, protective, hints at deep sorrow.

## 79. Pyrrhus, Scion of Aether (Mythic, Genderless)
- **Race/Class**: Interloper / Warlock (Aetherborn)
- **Combat Wear**: Cloak and vestments woven of pure light, antlerlike glowing horns, shifting, abstract runes.
- **Weapon**: Plasma wand, Aether sigils.
- **Loves**: Celestial riddles, chaos, discovery.
- **Hates**: Rules, predictability.
- **Lore**: Arrived through the Bleed, unfathomable even to demons—brings chaos and wild power.
- **Atmosphere**: Brilliant, unnerving, hypnotic.
- **Personality**: Childlike, unpredictable, cryptic—speaks in riddles.

## 80. Leonis, Sunlit Commander
- **Race/Class**: Human Betrayer / Vanguard
- **Combat Wear**: Sun-emblazoned cuirass, golden cape, red-plumed helm.
- **Weapon**: Spear of light, shield of the dawn.
- **Loves**: Chivalry, storytelling, morning duels.
- **Hates**: Slander, darkness.

- **Atmosphere**: Glorious, warm, formidable.
- **Personality**: Generous, bold, inspires greatness in others.

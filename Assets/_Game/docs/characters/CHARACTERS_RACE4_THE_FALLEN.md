# Maou Tower Defense: Vassals of Aethelgard — The Fallen (Celestial Outcasts)
**10 Characters (All Female)**

## 31. Seraphaine, Broken Dawn
- **Race/Class**: Fallen / Blood Sage
- **Combat Wear**: Filmy silver robes with golden embroidery, ragged "broken" halo, ethereal wings trailing feathers of light and shadow.
- **Weapon**: Staff crowned with a crystal sun.
- **Loves**: Sunrises after storm, children, gentle music.
- **Hates**: Cruelty under guise of faith, memories of betrayal.
- **Lore**: Once Luminary’s chief healer, cast out for showing compassion to demons.
- **Atmosphere**: Serene, radiant, with silent grief.
- **Personality**: Kind, self-sacrificing, quietly optimistic.

## 32. Eirielle, the Harrowed Grace
- **Race/Class**: Fallen / Ranger
- **Combat Wear**: Tattered blue and white tunic, thigh-high silver boots, halo of icy shards above platinum hair, large ashen wings.
- **Weapon**: Crystal longbow, frost-tipped arrows.
- **Loves**: Quiet moonlit nights, frozen lakes.
- **Hates**: False hope, being caged.
- **Lore**: Archangel scout who forsook the heavens for Aethelgard’s beauty and freedom.
- **Atmosphere**: Icy-cold, distant, exquisitely poised.
- **Personality**: Calm, watchful, slow to open her heart.

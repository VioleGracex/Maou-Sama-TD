# Vassal: Aquila
**Title**: The Harpy Queen  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon (Harpy)  
**Class**: Ranger  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Gold feathers, pointed ears.
- **Unique**: High-altitude fashion senses.

## Tactical Profile
Aquila is a magical ranged specialist. Her "Plume Storm" covers a wide area with razor-sharp feathers that deal continuous magic damage.

## Lore Fragment
Ruler of the Cloud-Spire kingdom. She views the surface world with a mix of pity and fascination. She joined the Legion to ensure the safety of the skies.

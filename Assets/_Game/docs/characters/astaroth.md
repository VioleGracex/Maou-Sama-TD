# Vassal: Astaroth
**Title**: Queen of Pain  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Executioner  

## Visual Identity
- **Wings**: Leathery shadow wings
- **Unique**: Manifests obsidian chains that lash out during combat.

## Tactical Profile
High-risk assassin. Astaroth deals massive critical damage but has low base HP. Her ultimate "Black Flame Crown" deals 450% damage to a single target and heals her for 20% of the damage dealt.

## Lore Fragment
The Citadel's chief enforcer. She is tasked with "cleaning" any insubordination within the demon ranks. She is fiercely protective of the Maou's dignity.

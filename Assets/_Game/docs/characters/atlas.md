# Vassal: Atlas
**Title**: The First Soul  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Familiar (Prime Titan)  
**Class**: Bastion (Familiar)  

## Visual Identity
- **Form**: Mountain-sized runic stone titan
- **Unique**: Moves so slowly he appears stationary.

## Tactical Profile
The ultimate blocker. Atlas has the highest HP and DEF in the game. He can block up to 5 units simultaneously and is immune to displacement effects.

## Lore Fragment
A primordial spirit bound to the Dark Throne since its foundation. He does not speak, but his presence acts as a grounding force for all demon magic.

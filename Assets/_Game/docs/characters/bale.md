# Vassal: Bale
**Title**: Ironclad Juggernaut  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Construct (Mech)  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Towering iron frame, obsidian plating.
- **Unique**: Infernal core visible in chest.

## Tactical Profile
The heaviest blocker in the game. Bale can block up to 4 units and has a "Blade-Cannon" that periodically fires for high physical damage.

## Lore Fragment
The ultimate achievement of the Citadel's artificers. He was built from the wreckage of a hundred Ironclad Union tanks.

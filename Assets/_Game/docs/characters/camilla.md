# Vassal: Camilla
**Title**: Ebon Lady of Chains  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Hair**: Silver with dark highlights
- **Unique**: Razor-sharp obsidian chains that she wields like whips.

## Tactical Profile
Stalling Vanguard. Camilla slows every unit she hits by 30% for 2 seconds. She is perfect for slowing down fast-moving boss units.

## Lore Fragment
A demon noble who spent a century in chains for refusing to bow to the previous Maou. She only serves the current Maou because they "have a more interesting soul."

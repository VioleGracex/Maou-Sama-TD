# Vassal: Hecate
**Title**: Mistress of Night  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon (Goddess)  
**Class**: Warlock  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Goat horns, gothic lolita dress.

## Tactical Profile
Hecate is a powerful crowd-control mage. Her "Midnight Stasis" freezes all enemies in a 4-tile radius for 5 seconds, allowing other units to safely pick them off.

## Lore Fragment
A goddess of the old world who prefers the "aesthetic" of the Dark Citadel to the sterile temples of the Luminary.

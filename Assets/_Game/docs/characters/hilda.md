# Vassal: Hilda
**Title**: The Living Fortress  
**Rarity**: SR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Tall, black horns.
- **Weapon**: Living shield with an eye that tracks intruders.

## Tactical Profile
Hilda is a standard heavy tank. Her high defense and block count make her ideal for basic lane defense. Her living shield provides a passive alert bonus to nearby units.

## Lore Fragment
A veteran of the Citadel Guard. She takes her duty seriously and refers to her shield as "Argus," claiming it never sleeps.

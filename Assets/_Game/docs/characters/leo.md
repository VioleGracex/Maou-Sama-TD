# Vassal: Leo
**Title**: The Sun Lion  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Shifter (Lion)  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Golden mane-like hair, feline fangs.
- **Unique**: Radiant fire aura.

## Tactical Profile
Leo is a high-damage brawler. His "Sun Lion Roar" deals massive AoE damage and stuns enemies briefly. His passive aura deals constant fire damage to any enemy he is blocking.

## Lore Fragment
Prince of the Sun-Lion tribe of the Southern Savanna. He seeks to prove that his people's strength is unmatched on the continent.

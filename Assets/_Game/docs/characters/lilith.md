# Vassal: Lilith
**Title**: Amethyst Sovereign  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Warlock  

## Visual Identity
- **Height**: 5'6"
- **Build**: Curvy / Elegant
- **Hair**: Platinum Blonde
- **Eyes**: Glowing Violet
- **Unique**: Obsidian horns curving back, translucent silk robes.

## Tactical Profile
Lilith is a magical nuker who provides crowd control. Her "Void Caress" deals significant AoE magic damage. On-hit, she has a chance to charm enemies, causing them to move away from the base temporarily.

## Lore Fragment
Succubus royalty and a renowned scholar of forbidden history. She views the war as a chess match played across the continent and considers the Maou her most talented opponent and favorite partner.

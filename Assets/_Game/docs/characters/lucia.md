
# Vassal: Lucia
**Title**: The Weeping Angel  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Class**: Sage  

## Visual Identity
- **Wings**: Tattered, ashen white, leaking golden particles.
- **Eyes**: perpetually weeping golden mana.
- **Unique**: A halo of sharp obsidian thorns.

## Tactical Profile
Lucia is the ultimate emergency healer. Her "Sinner Resurrection" skill is a global heal that can bring the entire Legion back from the brink of defeat. Her passive presence generates Authority Shards at an accelerated rate.

## Lore Fragment
A high-ranking seraph who was cast down for refusing to bless the "Holy Wall" that would have starved a demon civilian sector. She no longer prays to the Light; her devotion belongs to the throne of shadow.

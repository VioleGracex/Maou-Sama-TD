# Vassal: Magnus
**Title**: Iron Forgemaster  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Construct (Steel Golem)  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Build**: Massive muscular smith
- **Unique**: Flaming beard that pulses with the heat of his core.

## Tactical Profile
Mechanical synergy tank. Magnus buffs the HP and DEF of all adjacent "Construct" or "Architect" units by 20%.

## Lore Fragment
The head smith of the Obsidian Citadel. He forged most of the weapons used by the current generation of vassals.

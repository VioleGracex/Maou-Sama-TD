# The 13th Maou (Female) - Skills & Abilities
**Class**: Overlord (Caster / Tactician)
**Combat Style**: Magic Ranged
**Primary Weapon**: None (She channels dark matter and anti-magic directly through her hands, utilizing floating, geometric sigils that orbit her).

## Active Skills

### 1. Sovereign's Domain (Buff / Field Aura)
* **Description**: The Maou elegantly alters the local gravity and mana density in a massive radius around herself, asserting complete control over the battlefield's physical laws.
* **Effect**: Creates a persistent zone where all allied Ranged units gain increased Attack Range and Attack Speed. Simultaneously, any enemies that dare to enter the domain suffer drastically reduced Movement Speed and magic resistance.

### 2. Event Horizon (Single Target)
* **Description**: The Maou points a single, manicured finger at a high-value target, condensing dark matter into a microscopic singularity directly inside their core.
* **Effect**: The singularity wildly implodes, dealing devastating Single Target Magic Damage. If this strike successfully kills the target, the singularity collapses into a miniature black hole that pulls in nearby enemies without doing further damage, clumping them up for allied AOE strikes.

### 3. Star-Fall Requiem (AOE)
* **Description**: The Maou raises her hands lazily toward the sky, tearing open a rift to the void. She summons a localized, apocalyptic meteor shower of jagged dark-crystal projectiles.
* **Effect**: The crystals rain down over a designated, massive circular area. Deals continuous, heavy Magic Damage to all enemies caught inside over 5 seconds. Enemies hit are briefly Silenced, preventing them from casting their own abilities while they are being bombarded.

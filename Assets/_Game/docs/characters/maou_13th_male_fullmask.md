# The 13th Maou: Sovereign of Force (Male) - Full Mask
**Title**: The Sovereign of Force / The 13th Awakened  
**Rarity**: UR (Ultra Rare / Main Character)  
**Race**: Demon Lord  
**Class**: Overlord (Melee / Bruiser)  

## Visual Identity: Core Design
- **Height**: 6'3" (190cm)
- **Build**: Tall, slender, and impossibly elegant (a highly imposing bishounen/ikemen archetype). While he lacks the bulky muscle of a brute, his perfect posture and dominating presence make him tower over others. He moves with a predatory, effortless grace.
- **Hair**: Silky, silver-white hair that falls in soft, slightly messy layers just past his jawline, framing his face perfectly.
- **Eyes**: Striking, intense crimson with long, delicate eyelashes that offer a piercing, highly condescending gaze.
- **Attire**: High-fashion, tailored dark regalia that accentuates his slender, imposing frame. A sleek, midnight-blue fitted coat with silver embroidery, layered gracefully over an immaculate dress shirt with a popped collar. He wears elegant dark gloves and a flowing, asymmetrical half-cape.

---

## Mask Variation: The Monolith (Full-Face Blocking Mask)
* **The Look**: A terrifying, jagged starmetal helm that resembles a highly stylized, demonic skull. It features a crown of swept-back bladed horns and a predatory, aggressively fanged jawline pattern. Instead of standard eyeholes, a single, glowing vertical fissure runs down the center of the faceplate, blazing with concentrated, violent crimson mana.
* **The Vibe**: An intimidating, unstoppable supernatural juggernaut. When this helm goes on, the elegant prince vanishes entirely, replaced by a terrifying executioner radiating pure, suffocating malice.
* **Lore Context**: Worn only when deploying into the most brutal frontlines or when utterly abandoning his sanity for raw destructive power. The helm acts as a magical amplifier rather than a suppressor, forcing all his magical reserves outward to fuel his berserk state.

---

## Tactical Profile (Magic Melee)
The Sovereign of Force is an elite magic-melee Berserker vanguard. Though slender, he wields a massive, ornate "Soul-Severing Odachi" with one hand, channeling volatile crimson magic through the blade. He thrives in the center of the battlefield, shredding enemies with extremely rapid, magically enhanced sword strikes rather than relying on brute physical force alone.

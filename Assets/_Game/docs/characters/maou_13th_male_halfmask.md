# The 13th Maou: Sovereign of Force (Male) - Half Mask
**Title**: The Sovereign of Force / The 13th Awakened  
**Rarity**: UR (Ultra Rare / Main Character)  
**Race**: Demon Lord  
**Class**: Overlord (Melee / Bruiser)  

## Visual Identity: Core Design
- **Height**: 6'3" (190cm)
- **Build**: Tall, slender, and impossibly elegant (a highly imposing bishounen/ikemen archetype). While he lacks the bulky muscle of a brute, his perfect posture and dominating presence make him tower over others. He moves with a predatory, effortless grace.
- **Hair**: Silky, silver-white hair that falls in soft, slightly messy layers just past his jawline, framing his face perfectly.
- **Eyes**: Striking, intense crimson with long, delicate eyelashes that offer a piercing, highly condescending gaze.
- **Attire**: High-fashion, tailored dark regalia that accentuates his slender, imposing frame. A sleek, midnight-blue fitted coat with silver embroidery, layered gracefully over an immaculate dress shirt with a popped collar. He wears elegant dark gloves and a flowing, asymmetrical half-cape.

---

## Mask Variation: The Half-Visage (Eye-Covering Mask)
* **The Look**: A sleek, elegant domino mask forged from polished dark starmetal. It perfectly contours the upper half of his face, covering his eyes but leaving his aristocratic jawline and perfectly sculpted lips exposed—often pulled into an infuriatingly cocky smirk. It lacks any jagged edges, instead featuring smooth, aerodynamic curves.
* **The Vibe**: Untouchable, sassy, and devastatingly charming. He resembles a tragically romantic anti-hero who knows exactly how attractive and powerful he is, and uses it to completely mock his opponents.
* **Lore Context**: The standard attire he wears when holding court. The half-mask suppresses his volatile energy while allowing him to deliver highly sarcastic, charismatic commands that effortlessly secure the absolute (and sometimes obsessive) loyalty of his Vassals.

---

## Tactical Profile (Magic Melee)
The Sovereign of Force is an elite magic-melee Berserker vanguard. Though slender, he wields a massive, ornate "Soul-Severing Odachi" with one hand, channeling volatile crimson magic through the blade. He thrives in the center of the battlefield, shredding enemies with extremely rapid, magically enhanced sword strikes rather than relying on brute physical force alone. Detailed skill list can be found in his dedicated skills document.

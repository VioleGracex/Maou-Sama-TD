# The 13th Maou (Male) - Skills & Abilities
**Class**: Overlord (Vanguard / Berserker)
**Combat Style**: Magic Melee
**Primary Weapon**: The "Soul-Severing Odachi" - A massive, ornate, two-handed curved blade wielded effortlessly in one hand. It acts as a conduit for his highly volatile crimson mana.

## Active Skills

### 1. Tyrant's Awakening (Buff)
* **Description**: The Maou channels pure, concentrated crimson mana into his Odachi, wreathing the blade in dark, magical flames. 
* **Effect**: Significantly increases his own ATK and Attack Speed. While active, all of his basic attacks gain a massive Cleave effect, damaging all enemies in a wide arc in front of him. 
* **Drawback**: His overwhelming, berserk magic continuously drains his own HP while active.

### 2. Abyssal Guillotine (Single Target)
* **Description**: A focused, high-speed execution strike. The Maou vanishes into a burst of red mist and instantly reappears behind the highest-threat target, bringing his Odachi down in a catastrophic, concentrated vertical slash.
* **Effect**: Deals massive burst Magic Damage to a single target. This attack completely ignores enemy Armor and Shields, and instantly executes targets that are below a 20% health threshold.

### 3. Cataclysmic Grand Cross (AOE)
* **Description**: The Maou totally abandons precision for raw, destructive output. He steps forward with a terrifying roar and drives his Odachi straight deeply into the earth, pumping his crimson mana directly into the ground's ley lines.
* **Effect**: Gigantic pillars of crimson, magical fire erupt from the ground in a massive radius around him. Deals extreme sustained Magic Damage to all enemies caught in the blast and Stuns them for 2.5 seconds.

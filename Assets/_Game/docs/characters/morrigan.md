# Vassal: Morrigan
**Title**: Banefire Enchantress  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Warlock  

## Visual Identity
- **Eyes**: Reptilian slit pupils
- **Outfit**: Emerald-and-gold silk bodysuit
- **Unique**: Her mana staff ignites into a flaming spear.

## Tactical Profile
AoE Magic DPS. Her skill "Phantom Pyre" creates a 3x3 zone of fire on the battlefield that deals magic damage every 0.5s for 10 seconds.

## Lore Fragment
A mysterious sorceress who claims to have witnessed the birth of the 1st Maou. She treats the current war like a casual theatrical performance.

## 1. The Abyssal Fleet (Water/Eldritch)
**Theme**: Deep sea, pirate, bioluminescence, barnacles, ornate navy uniforms.

*   **Class**: Warlock
*   **Personality**: Ruthless and salt-crusted. He views the land-dwellers as "dry-rot."
*   **Right Hand**: **Trident of the Abyss** (Black metal, glowing blue core).
*   **Left Hand**: An ancient brass compass that points to the viewer.
*   **Outfit**: Heavily weathered navy blue admiral coat with gold-fringed epaulettes. No shirt underneath, revealing bioluminescent tattoos.

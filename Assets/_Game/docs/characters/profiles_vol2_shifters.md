# Legion Profiles Vol 2: The Silent Death & The Arcane

## [21-24] The Executioners (Part 2)

### 21. [SR] Page (The Paper-Cut)
*   **Origin**: Origami Spirit.
*   **Appearance**: Girl in a paper kimono with giant scissors.
*   **Demon Logic**: Her body can flatten into a sheet of paper to dodge.
*   **Appeal**: Quirky, gothic, "Creepy Doll" vibe.

### 22. [SR] Talon (The Falcon Sniper)
*   **Origin**: Avian Demon.
*   **Appearance**: Bird-eyes, talons, feathered "ear-wings."
*   **Demon Logic**: High ground damage bonus.
*   **Appeal**: Sharp, predatory, "High-Fashion Harpy."

### 23. [R] Viper (The Snake Assassin)
*   **Origin**: Naga Mutant.
*   **Appearance**: Snake-slit eyes, long green hair, iridescent scales.
*   **Demon Logic**: Attacks deal "Poison" over time.
*   **Appeal**: Sleek, dangerous, exotic.

### 24. [SSR] Nyx (The Void Walker)
*   **Origin**: Cosmic Horror.
*   **Appearance**: Woman composed of starlight and ink with pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Becomes invulnerable for 2s after every kill.
*   **Appeal**: Celestial, mysterious, "Star-Maiden."

## [25-32] The Rangers (Archers)

### 25. [SR] Shade (The Shadow Hunter)
*   **Origin**: Demon Ranger.
*   **Appearance**: Messy black hair, glowing green eyes, pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Arrows home in on targets.
*   **Appeal**: Loner, "Cool Archer" trope.

### 26. [SSR] Viper (The Magitech Sniper)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Reptilian eyes, magitech rifle, tactical gear.
*   **Demon Logic**: Longest range in the game; pierces armor.
*   **Appeal**: Professional, sleek, "Ghost in the Shell" vibes.

### 27. [R] Gale (The Wind Caller)
*   **Origin**: Harpy.
*   **Appearance**: Feathered ears, large wings, sharp eyes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Pushes enemies back with every shot.
*   **Appeal**: Energetic, wild, avian.

### 28. [SR] Stheno (The Serpent Bow)
*   **Origin**: Gorgon.
*   **Appearance**: Hair made of small vipers, snake pupils.
*   **Demon Logic**: Small chance to petrify (stun) targets.
*   **Appeal**: Dangerous beauty, mythological.

### 29. [SSR] Aquila (The Harpy Queen)
*   **Origin**: True Demon.
*   **Appearance**: Gold feathers, pointed ears, regal posture.
*   **Demon Logic**: Rain of feathers deals massive AoE magic damage.
*   **Appeal**: Empress, high-altitude fashion.

### 30. [SR] Orion (The Manticore Archer)
*   **Origin**: Beast Shifter.
*   **Appearance**: Lion ears, scorpion-tail braid.
*   **Demon Logic**: Poison arrows that stack.
*   **Appeal**: Exotic, wild, hunter aesthetic.

### 31. [R] Skye (The Winged Messenger)
*   **Origin**: Sparrow-kin.
*   **Appearance**: Small girl with big eyes and pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Increases attack speed of nearby Rangers.
*   **Appeal**: Cute, "Support Scout" vibe.

### 32. [SR] Bolt (The Storm Hunter)
*   **Origin**: Demon.
*   **Appearance**: Glowing cyan veins, rabbit ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Chain lightning attacks.
*   **Appeal**: Fast, electrifying, "Electric Bunny."

## [33-40] The Warlocks (Mages)

### 33. [SSR] Lilith (The Amethyst Sovereign)
*   **Origin**: Succubus Royalty.
*   **Appearance**: Obsidian horns, purple eyes, silk robes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Charms enemies to walk backwards for 3s.
*   **Appeal**: "Ara Ara" energy, elegant, seductive.

### 34. [SSR] Malakor (The Seraph of Ash)
*   **Origin**: Fallen Angel.
*   **Appearance**: Platinum hair, black blindfold, pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Summons rains of black ash that deal true damage.
*   **Appeal**: Ethereal, "Beautiful Destruction."

### 35. [SSR] Malphas (The Ink-Bound)
*   **Origin**: Demon Scholar.
*   **Appearance**: Ink-stained skin, three sets of ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Summons ink krakens from the ground.
*   **Appeal**: Intellectual, gothic, "Lovecraftian Beauty."

### 36. [SSR] Hecate (The Mistress of Night)
*   **Origin**: Demon Goddess.
*   **Appearance**: Goat horns, gothic lolita dress.
*   **Demon Logic**: Freezes time for all enemies in a large area.
*   **Appeal**: Dominant, "Gothic Queen."

### 37. [SR] Morgana (The Cursed Princess)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Glowing purple eyes, pointed ears, tattered royal dress.
*   **Demon Logic**: Curses enemies to take double damage from magic.
*   **Appeal**: Melancholy, corrupted nobility.

### 38. [SSR] Void (The Star-Eater)
*   **Origin**: Cosmic Spirit.
*   **Appearance**: Silhouette filled with moving nebula stars.
*   **Demon Logic**: Absorbs enemy mana to fuel her own ultimate.
*   **Appeal**: Abstract, celestial, "God-tier" aesthetic.

### 39. [SR] Neon (The Mana-Circuit)
*   **Origin**: Technomage.
*   **Appearance**: Cyan circuitry skin, pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Buffs the damage of nearby mechanical units.
*   **Appeal**: Cyberpunk, technical, futuristic.

### 40. [R] Mist (The Fog Sage)
*   **Origin**: Ghost.
*   **Appearance**: White hair, pale eyes, translucent robes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Creates a fog that makes allies untargetable.
*   **Appeal**: Ethereal, quiet, "Yuki-onna" vibe.

# Vassal: Selene
**Title**: Eclipse Duelist  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Human Betrayer  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Mask**: Veiled gold-and-black half-mask
- **Unique**: Twin rapiers that hum with lunar mana.

## Tactical Profile
Agile duelist. Selene has a high base dodge chance (15%). When she dodges, she immediately counter-attacks for 100% damage.

## Lore Fragment
A former champion of the Sanctus Empire's tournament. She defected after the high priests ordered her to assassinate a group of shifter children.

# Vassal: Shade
**Title**: Shadow Hunter  
**Rarity**: SR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Ranger  

## Visual Identity
- **Height**: 5'10"
- **Build**: Lean / Tactical
- **Hair**: Messy black (usually hooded)
- **Eyes**: Glowing Green
- **Unique**: Pointed ears, shadow-weave cloak.

## Tactical Profile
Shade is an efficient, low-cost physical ranged unit. He excels at picking off weakened enemies from high ground. His arrows have a slight homing property, making them reliable against fast moving targets.

## Lore Fragment
A pragmatist and survivor. Shade prefers the silence of the abyss to the hypocrisy of the surface world. He shares a friendly but intense rivalry with Ignis, often trying to one-up her tactical achievements.

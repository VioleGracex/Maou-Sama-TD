# The Bestiary of Aethelgard

## 1. The Holy Empire of Sanctus (Physical/Tank)
Sanctus units focus on durability and formation.
- **Paladin Elite**: High DEF, low speed. Can block 1 vassal (reversing the block mechanic).
- **Inquisitor**: Ranged magic. Silences Vassal skills on hit.
- **Saintess Bard**: Non-combatant. Creates a "Holy Circle" that heals 2% HP per second to allies.

## 2. The Ironclad Union (Tech/Artillery)
Ironclad units use machinery to bypass traditional magic.
- **Steam Golem (Boss)**: 2x2 footprint. Resistant to physical damage. Destroys traps on contact.
- **Mechanical Sapper**: Low HP, high speed. Ignores Vassals to target Towers/Traps.
- **Union Musketeer**: Extremely long range. Targets Vassals on High Ground first.

## 3. The Free Cities (Evasion/Assassin)
Mercenaries who focus on agility.
- **Duelist**: High dodge chance (30%). Can bypass 1 ground blocker per 10 seconds.
- **Bounty Hunter**: Targets the Maou directly. Ignores all path weights except distance to base.

## 4. The Frostbound Clans (Environment/Swarm)
Wild warriors from the north.
- **Tundra Wolf**: High speed. Jumps over 1-block Vassals.
- **Frost Shaman**: Creates "Ice Patches" that reduce Vassal ASPD by 20%.

## 5. The Interlopers (Isekai/Glitched)
Otherworlders with reality-bending powers.
- **Truck-kun (Boss)**: Straight-line charge. Deletes any unit with < 50% HP instantly.
- **The Protagonist**: Gains "Invulnerability" for 5 seconds upon first engagement.
- **Cheat Mage**: Casts spells that randomly shuffle the positions of deployed Vassals.

# The World of Aethelgard

## 1. The Conflict
For centuries, the Human Kingdoms of the West have expanded into the Dark Continent, claiming it is a "Holy Crusade". In reality, they seek the Mana Veins that run deep beneath the Demon lands.

## 2. The Demon King (Maou)
You are the **13th Maou**. The previous Maou, your father, fell in battle against the "Hero" Alaric. You have been resurrected by your loyal generals to reclaim the throne.
*   **Male Form**: Known as "The Obsidian Tyrant". Focuses on brute force and intimidation.
*   **Female Form**: Known as "The Violet Sovereign". Focuses on magical supremacy and political maneuvering.

## 3. Key Factions
*   **The Crimson Legion**: Led by General Ignis. The vanguard of the demon army. Values honor and strength.
*   **The Shadow Guild**: Led by Shade. Spies and assassins. Values results over methods.
*   **The Holy Empire**: Humans who wield "Light Magic" (actually simplified Mana). They view Demons as monsters to be exterminated.

## 4. Characters
*   **Ignis (The Crimson General)**: A war orphan raised by the previous Maou. Fiercely loyal but struggles with her feelings for the new Maou.
*   **Lilith (The Succubus Scholar)**: Possesses ancient knowledge. She treats the war like a chess game and enjoys teasing the Maou.

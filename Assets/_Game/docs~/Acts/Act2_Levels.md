# Act 2: The Crusade & The Summoning

**Setting:** The Trade Republic & Sanctus Empire
**Theme:** War & The Looming Threat
**Boss:** **The Summoned Hero (The Rival)** - Unwinnable/Survival Event

## Chapter 5: Expansion & Resources
*   **2-1: The Desert Trade Route**
    *   **Map Mechanic:** **Heat Wave** (Units take DoT unless near Oasis tiles).
    *   **Enemy:** **Sand Worms**.
*   **2-2: The Republic Gate**
    *   **Objective:** Siege Battle. Destroy the Gate HP.
    *   **Story:** You demand resources to fortify against the coming Hero. The Republic refuses. You take them.
*   **2-3: The False Hero's Parade**
    *   **Objective:** Intercept the caravan.
    *   **Story:** You see **Arthur** (The False Hero). He is weak. You realize it's a trap/distraction.

## Chapter 6: The Holy War
*   **2-4: The Crusade Begins**
    *   **Objective:** Defend against infinite waves for 5 minutes.
    *   **Enemy:** **Empire Paladin** (Bastion Class - High Magic Res).
*   **2-5: Night Raid**
    *   **Map Mechanic:** **Fog of War**.
*   **2-6: The Inquisitor**
    *   **Mini-Boss:** **High Inquisitor**.
    *   **Mechanic:** "Judgement" - Instantly kills the unit with the highest DPS. Bait it with a fodder unit.

## Chapter 7: The Sanctus Capital
*   **2-7: The White Walls**
    *   **Enemy:** **Angelic Construct** (Flying, ignores terrain).
    *   **Story:** You push to the capital to stop the Summoning Ritual.
*   **2-8: The Cathedral Plaza**
    *   **Objective:** Destroy the 4 Summoning Pylons.
    *   **Enemy:** **Zealot** (Explodes on death).

## Chapter 8: The Arrival (Climax)
*   **2-9: The Altar**
    *   **Boss:** **Arthur (The False Hero)**.
    *   **Mechanic:** Easy fight. He is a pushover.
    *   **Story:** You defeat Arthur easily. He cries out... and the sky cracks.
*   **2-10: The Rival Descends**
    *   **Boss:** **The Rival (True Hero)**.
    *   **Mechanic:** **SURVIVAL ONLY**.
        *   The Rival has infinite HP.
        *   **Twist:** As he charges his final attack, the sky flickers with **Static/Glitch effects** (Foreshadowing Act 3).
    *   **Story End:** You barely escape. The Hero destroys the capital. But Lilith notices the "Glitch" in the sky. "Something else is here..."

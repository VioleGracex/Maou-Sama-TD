# Act 3: The Inverse World & The Betrayal

**Setting:** Shattered Reality (Inverse World Invasion)
**Theme:** The World's Trap
**Boss:** **Avatar of the First Maou**

## Chapter 9: The Duel Interrupted
*   **3-1: The Clash**
    *   **Objective:** Defeat the Hero (The Rival).
    *   **Twist:** You drop the Hero to 1% HP. You try to deliver the killing blow.
    *   **The System Message:** **[PACT ACTIVATED: TARGET ESSENTIAL. LETHAL FORCE RESTRICTED.]**
    *   **Story:** Your attack bounces off. The sky tears open.
*   **3-2: The Invasion Begins**
    *   **Enemy:** **Inverse Horror** (Eldritch geometry).
    *   **Objective:** Protect the **Hero** (Who is stunned/confused).
    *   **Dialogue:**
        *   Maou: "DAMN YOU, WORLD! YOU TRICKED ME!"
        *   System: "Objective: Defend the Asset [Hero] against [Inverse World]."

## Chapter 10: The Unwilling Alliance
*   **3-4: Back to Back**
    *   **Objective:** Fight alongside the Hero.
    *   **Mechanic:** The Hero is an AI unit. If he dies, Game Over (Pact Violation).
    *   **Story:** The Hero realizes the First Maou is the real threat.
*   **3-6: The Origin**
    *   **Story:** You learn the First Maou created the Inverse World to delete "Gaia" and stop the cycle of suffering. Gaia used you to stop him.

## Chapter 11: The Counter-Attack
*   **3-7: The Rift Gate**
    *   **Objective:** Push into the Inverse World.
    *   **Enemy:** **Void Walker** (Assassin Class - Immune to physical dmg).

## Chapter 12: The First Maou (Climax)
*   **3-9: Boss Battle - Avatar of the First**
    *   **Phase 1: The Creator**
        *   The Boss summons "Deleted Timelines" (Glitch Enemeis).
    *   **Phase 2: The Binding**
        *   The Boss tries to delete the Hero. You must use **Sovereign Power: "Aegis"** to shield him.
        *   Maou: "I'm not saving you! I'm saving my chance to kill you later!"
    *   **Phase 3: The Banishment**
        *   The Hero uses "God Slayer" stroke. You amplify it with Authority.
    *   **Ending:** The First Maou is banished. The sky heals.
    *   **Epilogue:** The Pact binds you still. You cannot harm the Hero. You retreat to your castle, swearing to find a way to break the Pact and finish what you started.

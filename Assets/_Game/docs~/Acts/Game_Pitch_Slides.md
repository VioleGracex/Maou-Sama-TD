**Slide 1: Title Slide**
*   **Title:** Maou-Sama Tower Defense

**Slide 2: The Problem**
*   **Heading:** The Tower Defense Genre is Stagnant.
*   **Bullet Points:**
    *   Generic "Fantasy" or "Sci-Fi" themes are overused.
    *   Lack of meaningful narrative; soldiers are just disposable stats.
    *   Players feel like passive observers, not active commanders.
*   **Visual:** Screenshots of generic TD games with a "Boring" stamp overlay.

---

**Slide 3: The Solution**
*   **Heading:** Enter the "Villain Protagonist".
*   **Bullet Points:**
    *   **Role Reversal:** Play as the Raid Boss. Defend your dungeon from the "Heroes".
    *   **Character-Driven:** Your towers are *Vassals* with backstories, voice acting, and relationships.
    *   **Active Command:** The "Maou" casts global **Authority Seals**, changing the tide of battle directly.
*   **Visual:** Split screen. Left: Interesting character art. Right: Dynamic gameplay screenshot with a massive "Thunder Strike" spell effect.

---

**Slide 4: Core Gameplay Loop**
*   **Heading:** Battle. Bond. Build.
*   **Diagram:** A cycle flow chart.
    1.  **Preparation (Citadel):** Managing units, equipping gear, viewing story events.
    2.  **Combat (Defense):** Strategic grid-based tower defense. Stopping enemy waves.
    3.  **Reward (Summoning/Loot):** Earning currency to perform **Summoning Rituals** and upgrade the castle.
*   **Visual:** Three circular icons connected by arrows representing the loops.

# Maou-Sama TD: Currency System

The economy of *Maou-Sama TD* is built around two primary resources, distinguishing between gameplay progression and collection/summoning.

## 1. Gold (Common Currency)
**Icon**: Gold Coins
**Purpose**: General progression, upgrades, and social interactions.

- **Primary Uses**:
  - Upgrading units (leveling, skill upgrades).
  - Buying gifts for characters to increase loyalty/affinity.
  - Purchasing basic items from the in-game shop.
  - Base facility upgrades.
- **Acquisition**:
  - Battle rewards (Stage completion).
  - Daily mission rewards.
  - Selling unwanted basic items.
  - Passive income from certain base facilities.

## 2. Blood Crest (Premium Currency)
**Icon**: Crimson Seal/Crest
**Purpose**: High-value transactions and character acquisition (Summoning).

- **Primary Uses**:
  - Summoning new characters (Gacha System).
  - Buying premium skins or exclusive limited-time items.
- **Acquisition**:
  - First-time stage clears.
  - Milestone achievements.
  - Special events and login bonuses.
  - Premium purchases (Real money).

---

## Economy Balance Philosophy
*Maou-Sama TD* aims for a "Player-First" economy. We ensure that Gold is abundant enough for regular gameplay progression, while Blood Crests are distributed generously enough through gameplay to allow dedicated free-to-play players to participate in all major banners and systems.

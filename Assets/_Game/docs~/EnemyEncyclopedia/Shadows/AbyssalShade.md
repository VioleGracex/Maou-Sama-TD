# Abyssal Shade (Boss)

A high-ranking shadow entity capable of shifting its physical form into an ethereal state. It carries the weight of the Void's original silence.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow
- **Type**: Ground (Phasing)
- **Danger Level**: Very High (Boss)

## Abilities
- **Phasing Shift**: Can pass through physical objects and units for a short duration.
- **Ethereal Body**: Becomes completely immune to **Melee** damage while phasing.
- **Void Pulse**: Releases a wave of cold energy that slows nearby defenders.

## Tactics
Wait for its **Phasing** state to end or use **Sovereign Rites** and **Magic** to bypass its physical immunity.

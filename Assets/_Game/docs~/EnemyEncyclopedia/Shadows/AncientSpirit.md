# Ancient Spirit

The tormented soul of a long-dead cultist or minor demon. It floats wrapped in tattered robes, attacking from a distance with cold, necrotic energy.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow / Ethereal
- **Type**: Floating Ranged
- **Danger Level**: Medium

## Abilities
- **Necrotic Bolt**: A ranged projectile that deals magical damage and drains mana.
- **Ethereal Body**: Can float over some ground obstacles.

## Lore
"Their whispers are more dangerous than their bolts. They speak of a home that no longer exists." — Hecatina

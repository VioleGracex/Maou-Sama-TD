# Lesser Shadow

The most common manifestation of the Void's hunger. These creatures lack individual will, acting as a simple extension of the darkness.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow
- **Type**: Ground Melee
- **Danger Level**: Low

## Abilities
- **Void Strike**: A minor physical swipe that drains a small amount of vitality.

## Lore
"They are but the foam on the wave of darkness. Individually weak, but in numbers, they can drown even the brightest light." — Hecatina

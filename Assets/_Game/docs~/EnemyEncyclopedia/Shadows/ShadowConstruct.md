# Shadow Construct

A humanoid shape roughly formed from coalesced shadows. It mimics the form of a soldier but remains unstable, constantly shifting and dripping shadow-matter.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow
- **Type**: Ground Warrior
- **Danger Level**: Medium

## Abilities
- **Shifting Blade**: Its limbs can morph into jagged spikes or blades at will.
- **Unstable Core**: Occasional bursts of shadow energy when damaged.

## Lore
"They are crude attempts at mimicry. The Void trying to remember what a soldier looks like, only to fail in every detail but the violence." — Hecatina

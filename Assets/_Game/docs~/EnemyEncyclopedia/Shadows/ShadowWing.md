# Shadow Wing

Agile aerial hunters that bypass ground defenses. They appear as tattered silhouettes against the moon, swooping down with unnatural speed.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow
- **Type**: Flying
- **Danger Level**: Medium

## Abilities
- **Aerial Bypass**: Completely ignores ground-based blockers.
- **Swoop Attack**: Delivers a quick strike before rising back out of reach of most melee units.

## Tactics
Requires **Ranged** or **Magic** units to engage effectively.

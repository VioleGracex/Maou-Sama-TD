# Tomb Guardian

An ancient suit of armor possessed by shadow energy. Heavy, slow, and incredibly durable, it acts as a sentinel for the tomb's deepest secrets.

## Profile
- **Race**: Shadow / Possessed
- **Type**: Ground Tank
- **Danger Level**: High

## Abilities
- **Heavy Plating**: High physical resistance based on the possessed armor.
- **Relentless March**: Cannot be easily pushed back or slowed by minor strikes.

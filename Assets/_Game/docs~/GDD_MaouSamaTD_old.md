# Maou-Sama TD: Game Design Document (Master)

## 1. Game Overview
**Maou-Sama TD** is a high-fidelity Gacha Tower Defense game with Visual Novel storytelling. Players inherit the mantle of the **13th Demon Lord (Maou)**, tasked with defending the Abyssal Citadel against the relentless crusades of "Heroic" forces and celestial invaders.

---

## 2. Core Mechanics

### A. Tower Defense Combat
- **Lane-Based Strategy**: Defense occurs on multi-lane maps with distinctive Ground and High-Ground tiles.
- **Unit Types**:
  - **Melee (Blockers)**: Placed on ground tiles to stop enemy advance.
  - **Ranged (Snipers/Mages)**: Placed on high ground; prioritize flying or specific enemy types.
  - **Support/Vassals**: Buff allies or generate resources.
- **Maou Skills & Deployment**: Manual player spells (Thunder Strike, Rally, Terror) and unit deployment consume **Authority Seals**.

### B. Progression & Gacha
- **Summoning (Vassal Contracts)**: Players summon "Vassals" (Demon Generals, Monsters, and Betrayed Heroes) using **Blood Crests**.
- **Growth Systems**:
  - **Leveling**: Uses **Gold** and XP items.
  - **Awakening**: Duplicate-based star increases.
  - **Affection/Loyalty**: Building bonds using **Gifts** and Gold to unlock secret CGs and stat bonuses.

---

## 3. Game Modes
1. **Campaign (The Eternal War)**: Linear story progression with VN segments.
2. **Daily Dungeons**: 
   - *Gold Vault*: Economy focus.
   - *Exp Forest*: Growth focus.
   - *Material Mine*: Evolution materials.
3. **Tower of Babel**: 100-floor challenge with persistent HP and monthly resets.
4. **Social Hub**: Friend Support units and Time-Attack Leaderboards.

---

## 4. Narrative & Lore
- **Setting**: The Multiverse, centered on the **Abyssal Citadel**.
- **The Player**: The 13th Maou. Your identity is adaptable (Male/Female).
- **Major Factions**:
  - **Trueborn Demons**: The original inhabitants of the Abyss.
  - **The Fallen**: Former Angels or Clerics who chose the dark.
  - **Human Betrayers**: Mortal generals who saw the "light" was a lie.
- **Key Conflict**: The "Graceful Crusade" seeks to wipe the Abyss from existence. The 13th Maou must unite the fractured demon clans to survive.

---

## 5. Economy & Currencies
- **Gold**: Common currency for upgrading units, buying gifts, and shop transactions.
- **Blood Crests**: Premium currency used exclusively for the Gacha (Summoning) system.
- **Authority Seals**: In-battle currency used for unit deployment and activating Maou Skills.

# Act 1: The Tomb of True Demons

## Scene 1: The Awakening Ritual

The world smelled of ozone and stale blood.

"Calibration complete. Mana density at 98%. Soul anchor... stabilized."

The voice was cool, efficient, and utterly devoid of panic, despite the sounds of battle echoing from the corridor.

You open your eyes.

You are lying on a cold stone slab. Above you, a circle of floating runic monitors glows with pale blue light. A woman stands over you, adjusting a holographic display on her tactical slate. She wears a sharp, military-style uniform of black silk, her posture rigid.

**Hecatina (The Dark Seneschal)**: "Welcome back to the waking world, my Liege. I am Hecatina. I have successfully completed the Rite of Return."

She bows, precise and deep, but immediately returns to her screen.

**Hecatina**: "I apologize for the lack of fanfare. The resurrection process took longer than calculated, and our position is... compromised."

A loud *CRASH* echoes from the hallway, followed by the roar of flames.

**Ignis (Off-screen)**: "HOLD THE LINE! Not a single one of you filths gets past me!"

**Hecatina**: "That would be Ignis. She is currently holding back a swarm of Lesser Shadows attempting to interrupt your nap. She is capable, but the numbers are unfavorable."

She looks at you, her eyes sharp behind rimless glasses.

**Hecatina**: "You are the 13th Sovereign. Your power is currently... negligible. However, your Authority remains. Ignis needs your command to unleash her full potential. Please, extend your will."

## Scene 2: The Second Awakening

With the immediate threat cleared, Hecatina guides you deeper into the tomb.

**Hecatina**: "Ignis will secure the rear. We must proceed to the inner sanctum. My calculations suggest we have enough residual mana to awaken one more vassal before the main wave arrives."

You arrive at a sealed sarcophagus wrapped in thorny vines of red energy.

**Hecatina**: "The Duchess of Crimson Allure. A... risky choice, but her combat power is undeniable."

She taps a sequence into her tablet. The vines wither. The stone lid slides open with a hiss.

From the darkness, a hand reaches out—pale, elegant, with manicured nails painted the color of fresh blood.

**Lilith**: "Mmm... five hundred years... was it?"

A woman rises. She is breathtaking, wearing a dress that seems woven from desire itself. Her horns curve gracefully, and her wings unfurl like petals. She blinks, her crimson eyes scanning the room until they land on you.

Her smile instantly changes from lazy to predatory.

**Lilith**: "Oh? Can it be?"

She steps out of the coffin, ignoring the dust, and glides towards you. She stops inches away, her scent like roses and iron.

**Lilith**: "My Master... My beautiful, cruel Master. You've finally come for me."

She falls to her knees, grasping your hand and pressing it to her cheek.

**Lilith**: "I dreamt of you. Every. Single. Night."

**Hecatina**: *(Clearing throat loudly)* "Duchess Lilith. Please refrain from contaminating the Sovereign with grave dust. We are in a combat zone."

Lilith glares at Hecatina, her eyes narrowing.

**Lilith**: "Hecatina. Still the fun-killing little calculator, I see. Can't you see I'm having a moment with *our* Lord?"

**Hecatina**: "I see you wasting time. The Shadow of a True Demon approaches. Should I designate you as 'Decoy' in the battle plan?"

**Lilith**: *(Chuckles darkly)* "Decoy? Darling, I'll turn that shadow into a rug for the Master's throne room."

She turns back to you, winking.

**Lilith**: "Command me, Master. Let me remind this tomb who truly owns the dark."

# Animation Prompts: Ignis Ultimate (Phoenix Summon)

Use these prompts with an AI image generator to create consistent frames for Ignis's **Ultimate** animation.

## Character Consistency Guidelines
- **Subject**: Female knight, 5'11", athletic build, red crimson heavy armor, gold filigree.
- **Hair**: Long scarlet ponytail.
- **Features**: Amber eyes, obsidian crown horns manifested on forehead.
- **Weapon**: Crimson Sovereign’s Reach (Regal red longsword, ruby core, silver edge).
- **Style**: 2D Anime Sprite Art, High Quality, Clean Lineart, Vibrant Colors, Fantasy TD Style.

---

### Frame 1: The Charge (Preparation)
> **Prompt**: Full body 2D anime sprite of Ignis the red knight, low crouch stance, holding red longsword with both hands at her side, ruby core of sword glowing intensely, embers swirling around her feet, obsidian horns glowing, white background, clean cel-shaded style.

### Frame 2: The Raise (Sword Upwards)
> **Prompt**: Full body 2D anime sprite of Ignis the red knight, standing tall, raising her red longsword vertically towards the sky with both hands, eyes glowing amber, a pillar of fire erupting from the ground around her, scarlet ponytail flowing upwards, white background, cinematic lighting.

### Frame 3: The Manifestation (Phoenix Appears)
> **Prompt**: Full body 2D anime sprite of Ignis the red knight, sword held high, a massive ethereal phoenix made of fire and golden light manifesting behind her, wings spread wide, flame trails connecting sword to phoenix, majestic and powerful, white background, detailed fire effects.

### Frame 4: The Release (Summoning Burst)
> **Prompt**: Full body 2D anime sprite of Ignis the red knight, sword pointing forward, the fire phoenix diving past her towards the viewer, explosion of embers and light, fierce expression, hair whipping in the wind, white background, dynamic action pose.

---

### Tips for Better Results
- **Negative Prompt**: (bad anatomy, blurry, low quality, messy lines, complex background, wings on character, shield).
- **Aspect Ratio**: Use `--ar 1:1` for sprites.
- **Consistency**: Use the same "Seed" if your generator supports it to keep her face and armor the same across frames.

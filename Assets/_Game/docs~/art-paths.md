# Art Asset Directory & AI Prompting Specs

## 1. Technical AI Prompting Guide
To maintain style across 60 assets, use these "Negative Prompts" and "Style Modifiers":

*   **Global Style Modifier**: `(HoYo anime style:1.2), (Masterwork:1.2), Sharp outlines, flat colors, high contrast, clean background.`
*   **Negative Prompts**: `(3d:1.5), realistic, blurry, messy lines, low-res, multiple characters, text, signature, western art style.`

## 2. Form Logic Prompts
*   **Humanoid Form**: `Humanoid, ears/horns/tails optional, elegant clothing, noble posture.`
*   **Demon Form**: `Monstrous features, glowing eyes, shadow aura, aggressive pose, transformation effects.`

## 3. Directory Mapping
*   `public/images/characters/cards/`: (3:4) thumbnails.
*   `public/images/characters/portraits/`: (5:7) transparent sprites for dialogue.
*   `public/images/characters/chambers/`: (16:9) Background CGs for Royal Chambers.
*   `public/images/characters/sprites/`: (1:1) Isometric battle units.

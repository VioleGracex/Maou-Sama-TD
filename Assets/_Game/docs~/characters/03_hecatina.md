## Tactical Profile
Hecatina does not engage in direct combat. Her presence on the field (usually as a HUD element) provides passive regeneration for Authority Seals and increases the effective range of all deployed vassals.

## Lore Fragment
Your closest advisor and the actual brain behind the Citadel's logistics. She was created by the previous Maou specifically to guide the successor. She treats the player with "reverent authority," though she isn't afraid to provide blunt strategic critiques.

## Mask Variation: Unmasked
* **The Look**: The masquerade mask is completely removed. Without the obstruction, her face is flawlessly beautiful, serene, and almost angelic—a stark contrast to her title as Demon Lord. During intense magical exertion, a delicate, glowing sigil pulses faintly on her forehead like a tiara of light.
* **The Vibe**: Vulnerable, intimately authentic, and devastatingly gorgeous. Removing the mask drops her untouchable "chess master" persona, revealing the beautiful, burdened woman bearing the weight of demonkind.
* **Lore Context**: This state is reserved strictly for moments of massive spellcasting (where she needs zero mana impedance) or when she is entirely alone with her closest, most trusted confidants (like Lilith or Ignis). Seeing her unmasked is considered the highest honor a Vassal can receive.

## Mask Variation: Unmasked 
* **The Look**: The mask is completely removed revealing flawlessly sculptured, tragically beautiful features. He has a calm, intensely dominant gaze that draws people in while simultaneously making them feel small. Without the mask suppressing his identity, a subtle, elegant glowing crest (the mark of the 13th) becomes visible on his cheekbone, adding to his dangerous mystique.
* **The Vibe**: Intoxicatingly handsome, fiercely imposing, and arrogant. Removing the mask strips away the playful trickster persona, revealing a deeply romanticized but intensely terrifying leader who knows he holds the world in the palm of his hand.
* **Lore Context**: He removes the mask during his "Ultimate/Awakened" combat state to end fights quickly. He also goes unmasked only in front of his most trusted, intimately close Lieutenants, reserving his rare moments of genuine (if still slightly sassy) exhaustion for them alone.

## Tactical Profile (Magic Melee)
The Sovereign of Force is an elite magic-melee Berserker vanguard. Though slender, he wields a massive, ornate "Soul-Severing Odachi" with one hand, channeling volatile crimson magic through the blade. He thrives in the center of the battlefield, shredding enemies with extremely rapid, magically enhanced sword strikes rather than relying on brute physical force alone.

# Maou Tower Defense: Vassals of Aethelgard — Husbando Apex & Legendary Allies
**10 Characters (All Male; the most iconic male vassals and two legendary genderless familiars)**

---

## 61. Balthazar, Lord of Sloth
- **Race/Class**: Trueborn Demon / Warlock
- **Combat Wear**: Flowing obsidian robe, polished horns, golden eye chain, mantle of shadows.
- **Weapon**: Staff of Dreams, chain of midnight essence.
- **Loves**: Luxurious beds, clever conversation.
- **Hates**: Early mornings, hasty plans.
- **Lore**: Oldest of the Demon Lords, said to see all that happens in Maou’s domain in his dreams.
- **Atmosphere**: Heavy, languid, intimidatingly calm.
- **Personality**: Lethargic, genuinely wise, dry humor. Unstoppable when roused.

---
## 62. Fenris, Wolf King of the North
- **Race/Class**: Shifter (Lupine) / Bastion
- **Combat Wear**: Massive gray-furred armor, wolf fangs patterned on shoulders, royal blue cloak.
- **Weapon**: Double-headed greataxe, claws.
- **Loves**: Loyalty, duels, fresh meat.
- **Hates**: Betrayal, cowardice.
- **Lore**: Ancient ruler of the wolf tribes, fights for the Maou who defeated him in single combat.
- **Atmosphere**: Thunderous, primal.
- **Personality**: Whenever not fighting, surprisingly gentle. Fiercely protective.

---
## 63. Victor, Fallen Paladin
- **Race/Class**: Human Betrayer / Vanguard
- **Combat Wear**: White ruined plate, blue sashes, jagged holy sigils crossed out.
- **Weapon**: Two-handed blessed sword, shield with faded crest.
- **Loves**: Redemption, honor, honest drink.
- **Hates**: Hypocrisy, cowards.
- **Lore**: Led failed rebellion against Sanctus, now the Maou’s unwavering shield.
- **Atmosphere**: Stoic, quietly haunted.
- **Personality**: Loyal, gruff, holds tight to ideals despite his past fall.

---
## 64. Raphael, Golden Exile
- **Race/Class**: The Fallen / Tactician
- **Combat Wear**: High-collared white armor, massive tattered gold wings, unbroken halo.
- **Weapon**: Platinum polearm, command scroll.
- **Loves**: Strategems, music, kindness.
- **Hates**: Zealotry, loss of innocence.
- **Lore**: Most talented strategist cast out for refusing a holy order; a legend to outcasts.
- **Atmosphere**: Serene, radiant, magnetic.
- **Personality**: Compassionate, idealistic, always sees the big picture.

---
## 65. Lucien, Phantom Operative
- **Race/Class**: Undead (Phantom) / Assassin
- **Combat Wear**: Shadowlined dark tuxedo, mist-shrouded figure, half-luminous mask.
- **Weapon**: Dual ghost blades, smoke bombs.
- **Loves**: Masquerades, secrets, the moon.
- **Hates**: Impatience, harsh lights.
- **Lore**: Master infiltrator and dancer, protector of immortal secrets.
- **Atmosphere**: Elegant, finely elusive.
- **Personality**: Courteous, flirty, loves games.

---
## 66. Magnus, Iron Forgemaster
- **Race/Class**: Construct (Steel Golem) / Bastion
- **Combat Wear**: Ironwork armor with runes, steam-driven joints, flaming beard, masterwork hammer.
- **Weapon**: Master forge hammer, rune shield.
- **Loves**: Craftsmanship, ale, molten metal.
- **Hates**: Shoddy work, cold water.
- **Lore**: Woke to defend the Maou’s forges; crafts the weapons for the Legion.
- **Atmosphere**: Booming, jovial.
- **Personality**: Gregarious, competitive, dislikes idleness.

---
## 67. Kael, Dragonblood Duelist
- **Race/Class**: Mythological Hybrid (Dragonoid) / Vanguard
- **Combat Wear**: Scale mail, open crimson jacket, claw rings, tail.
- **Weapon**: Twin sabers, fire runes.
- **Loves**: Glory, flying, spicy food.
- **Hates**: Losing, cold, disrespect.
- **Lore**: Reputedly sired by an ancient dragon, boastful yet big-hearted.
- **Atmosphere**: Smoldering, fearless, charismatic.
- **Personality**: Loud, showy, fiercely motivates others.

---
## 68. Eidon, Archlich Supreme
- **Race/Class**: Undead (Lich) / Warlock
- **Combat Wear**: Platinum boneplate, drifting blue spirit flames, crown of black ice.
- **Weapon**: Scepter of eternal frost.
- **Loves**: Lost knowledge, loyalty, stargazing.
- **Hates**: Ignorance, petty revenge.
- **Lore**: Ancient king-lich who once ruled the north; eager for Maou’s leadership.
- **Atmosphere**: Icy, grave, awe-inspiring.
- **Personality**: Patient, kindly to those who respect learning, ruthless to his foes.

---
## 69. Orion, Maestro of Whispers
- **Race/Class**: Familiar (Celestial Imp) / Support
- **Combat Wear**: Star-motif waistcoat, floating scarf, always glows faintly, gold bells.
- **Weapon**: Melodic flute, spell scrolls.
- **Loves**: Lullabies, bedtime stories, secrets.
- **Hates**: Suffering, loud noises.
- **Lore**: Guardian spirit of the Demon King’s children; his music buffs or soothes all nearby.
- **Atmosphere**: Sleepy, warm, mysterious.
- **Personality**: Soft-spoken, caring, good listener, clever with words.

---
## 70. Zephyr, Eternal Wind (Genderless Legendary Familiar)
- **Race/Class**: Familiar (Mythic Sprite) / Ranger
- **Combat Wear**: Airy translucent vest, drifting veils, sparkling wings, not truly humanoid.
- **Weapon**: Whistling windblade, zephyr arrows.
- **Loves**: Cloud-watching, laughter, helping new adventurers.
- **Hates**: Stone walls, sadness.
- **Lore**: Oldest wind spirit, stories say it taught the first Demon King to soar.
- **Atmosphere**: Playful, wild, forever free.
- **Personality**: Innocent, wise, easily amused, always encouraging.

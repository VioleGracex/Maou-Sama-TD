# Maou Tower Defense: Vassals of Aethelgard — Trueborn Demons (Abyssal Nobility)
**10 Characters (All Female)**

---

## 1. Lilith, Duchess of Crimson Allure
- **Race/Class**: Trueborn Demon / Warlock (Succubus)
- **Combat Wear**: Silken, high-slit crimson dress with armored bustier, obsidian pauldrons; spectral wings of deep red mana.
- **Weapon**: Arcane scepter topped with a blood-red gem.
- **Loves**: Devotion, elegance, beautiful music.
- **Hates**: Betrayal, crudeness, disorder.
- **Lore**: Legendary temptress and strategist, rumored to have charmed emperors and devils alike.
- **Atmosphere**: Bewitching, noble, mysterious; voice like velvet.
- **Personality**: Seductive but surprisingly earnest, fiercely loyal to Maou-sama.

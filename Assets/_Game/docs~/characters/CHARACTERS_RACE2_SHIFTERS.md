## 11. Lupa, Alpha Huntress
- **Race/Class**: Lupine Shifter / Vanguard
- **Combat Wear**: Grey-furred hooded cloak, leather breastplate, armored boots; wolf tail and pointed ears prominent.
- **Weapon**: Twin curved short swords.
- **Loves**: Loyalty, pack camaraderie, the wild hunt.
- **Hates**: Betrayal, caging, encroachment on her forest.
- **Lore**: Revered by beast-kin clans, known for fierce wolfpack tactics; once broke through Holy Empire lines.
- **Atmosphere**: Earthy, primal, charged with energy.
- **Personality**: Protective, brash, fiercely loyal to pack and the Maou.

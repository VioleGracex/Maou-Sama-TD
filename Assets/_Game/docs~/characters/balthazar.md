# Vassal: Balthazar
**Title**: Lord of Sloth  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Horns**: Massive curled ram horns
- **Style**: High-fashion silk robes
- **Unique**: Perpetually sleepy expression even while fighting.

## Tactical Profile
Crowd Control Tank. Balthazar doesn't deal much damage but his "Lazy Aura" passively slows all enemies within a 2-tile radius by 20%.

## Lore Fragment
One of the original Demon Lords. He claims that managing the Citadel's budget is more exhausting than fighting a holy crusade.

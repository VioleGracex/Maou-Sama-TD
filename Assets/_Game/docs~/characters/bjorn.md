# Vassal: Bjorn
**Title**: Frostbound Colossus  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Shifter (Half-Giant)  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Size**: Giant-kin
- **Tattoos**: Glowing blue frost runes
- **Weapon**: Mammoth Warhammer

## Tactical Profile
The ultimate high-cost tank. Bjorn can block up to 4 enemies simultaneously. His "Glacial Impact" skill stuns enemies in a 2-tile radius for a significant duration, making him perfect for stopping elite waves.

## Lore Fragment
Warchief of the Northern Clans. He only joined the Legion after the Maou defeated him in a three-day duel. He respects strength above all and views his service to the Dark Throne as a debt of honor.

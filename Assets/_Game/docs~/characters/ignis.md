# Vassal: Ignis
**Title**: The Crimson Bastion  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Human Betrayer  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Height**: 5'11"
- **Build**: Athletic / Knightly
- **Hair**: Long scarlet ponytail
- **Eyes**: Amber
- **Unique**: Obsidian "Crown Horns" manifested in combat.

## Tactical Profile
Ignis is the definitive ground blocker. She excels at holding chokepoints against multiple units. Her Obsidian Aegis skill provides a massive defensive buff to her and nearby allies.

## Lore Fragment
A former Saint Knight of the Sanctus Empire. She defected after witnessing the "Holy Crusade" target innocent demon civilians. She now serves the 13th Maou with a stoic, absolute devotion that borders on obsession.

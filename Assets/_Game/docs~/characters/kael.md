# Vassal: Kael
**Title**: Dragonblood Duelist  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Mythological Hybrid (Dragonoid)  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Hair**: Crimson spiky hair
- **Unique**: Red dragon scales visible on his arms and neck.

## Tactical Profile
High-speed fire specialist. Kael's fire rate is extremely high, and every 5th attack triggers an explosion that deals small AoE damage.

## Lore Fragment
Said to be the offspring of a Great Red Dragon and a human hero. He joined the Maou because he found the Holy Empire's "Hero Propaganda" to be boring.

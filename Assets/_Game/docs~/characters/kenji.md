# Vassal: Kenji
**Title**: Glitched Hero  
**Rarity**: SSR (Interloper)  
**Race**: Interloper (Terra Prime)  
**Class**: Gunner  

## Visual Identity
- **Style**: Modern Techwear
- **Gear**: VR Goggles, Holographic Pistols

## Tactical Profile
A unique "Interloper" class unit. Kenji deals True Damage (ignoring armor/RES). His fire rate is the highest in the game, but he consumes a small amount of Authority per shot, making him a high-risk, high-reward tactical asset.

## Lore Fragment
Summoned from 21st-century Tokyo by the Holy Empire, Kenji quickly realized they were "NPC fanatics" and defected to the Maou's side because the Citadel had "better aesthetics and snacks." He refers to the world's physics as "broken code."

# Vassal: Lilith
**Title**: Matriarch of Lust  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Warlock  

## Visual Identity
- **Height**: 5'6"
- **Build**: Curvy / Voluptuous / Elegant
- **Hair**: Pure Blonde
- **Skin**: Creamy Pale
- **Eyes**: Glowing Violet
- **Unique**: Thin, elegant obsidian horns curving back. She wears a form-fitting, elegant ensemble in alluring red and violet, complemented by matching opera-length gloves and sheer dark stockings that accentuate her mature, curvy silhouette.

## Weapon: The Obsidian Mirror of Avarice
- **Type**: Floating Hexagonal Catalyst
- **Visuals**: A multifaceted mirror that orbits her palm, refracting ambient light into violet soul-bolts.
- **Function**: Concentrates her authority into piercing projectiles. During her ultimate, it fragments to blanket the area in dark energy.

## Tactical Profile
Lilith is a magical nuker and master of battlefield manipulation. She radiates an alluring but dangerous aura that draws enemies in, only to crush them with "Void Caress". On-hit, her charms are so potent that enemies lose their will to fight, wandering aimlessly away from the base in a daze.

## Lore Fragment
A high-ranking Trueborn and Matriarch of the Lust lineage. Lilith isn't just a scholar; she's a master of the "long game". She carries the matured elegance of an immortal who has seen empires rise and fall, viewing the current conflict with a smug, motherly condescension. She treats the Maou with a mix of fierce loyalty and predatory teasing, often referring to the Sovereign as her "favorite little spark."

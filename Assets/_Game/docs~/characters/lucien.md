# Vassal: Lucien
**Title**: Phantom Operative  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Undead (Phantom)  
**Class**: Executioner  

## Visual Identity
- **Outfit**: Shadowlined dark tuxedo, mist-shrouded figure.
- **Weapon**: Dual ghost blades.

## Tactical Profile
Lucien is a high-speed assassin. His "Ghost Waltz" allows him to strike multiple times in a split second, dealing massive dark damage. His high natural dodge makes him surprisingly resilient for an Executioner.

## Lore Fragment
A master infiltrator who protected immortal secrets for centuries. He joined the Maou's Legion to find a "stage" worthy of his talents.

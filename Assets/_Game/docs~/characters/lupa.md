
# Vassal: Lupa
**Title**: Alpha Huntress  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Hair**: Silvery gray, wild and layered.
- **Eyes**: Piercing yellow with bestial slits.
- **Features**: Lupine ears and a thick bushy tail.
- **Unique**: Wears a hooded cloak made from the fur of an ancient alpha she defeated.

## Tactical Profile
Lupa is a high-pressure vanguard who specializes in bleeding targets. Her "Pack Call" skill buffs the attack power of all allies in her immediate vicinity, making her a perfect centerpiece for a physical cohort.

## Lore Fragment
Once the leader of the Moon-Eater tribe, Lupa pledged her life to the 13th Maou after being saved from an Imperial purge. She views the Legion as her new pack and will sacrifice anything to ensure their survival.

# Vassal: Malina
**Title**: Infernal Countess  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Bastion  

## Visual Identity
- **Hair**: Raven black
- **Armor**: Black lacquer with sharp horned helmet
- **Unique**: Molten shield that drips demon-fire.

## Tactical Profile
An absolute defensive unit. Malina increases the DEF of all allies in adjacent tiles by 15%. Her skill allows her to block an additional unit for 10 seconds.

## Lore Fragment
A general from the First Era who slept in the volcanic depths until the 13th Maou's awakening. She values discipline above all else.

# The 13th Maou: Sovereign of Guile (Female) - Full Mask
**Title**: The Sovereign of Guile / The 13th Awakened  
**Rarity**: UR (Ultra Rare / Main Character)  
**Race**: Demon Lord  
**Class**: Overlord (Caster / Tactician)  

## Visual Identity: Core Design
- **Height**: 5'9" (175cm)
- **Build**: Slender, elegant, and breathtakingly beautiful. She carries herself with pristine, regal posture that demands the entire room's attention without uttering a word. 
- **Hair**: Silky midnight black that catches the light with a faint violet sheen. It is styled perfectly straight, cascading flawlessly down to her lower back.
- **Eyes**: A mesmerizing amethyst purple, clear and piercing, devoid of monstrous traits but intensely commanding.
- **Attire**: A breathtakingly sophisticated gothic ballgown interwoven with practical metallic threads and dark silk. It hugs her figure elegantly and flows seamlessly when she moves, giving the illusion that she is gliding rather than walking.

---

## Mask Variation: The Eclipse (Full-Face Blocking Mask)
* **The Look**: A completely smooth, reflective silver-and-black faceplate built directly over a hood. It has absolutely zero features—no eyes, no lips, no contours. It is a literal mirror of polished dark glass. The mask is hauntingly beautiful in its minimalist perfection, devoid of any jagged or demonic aesthetics. 
* **The Vibe**: Divine, alien, and flawlessly unapproachable. She looks less like a character and more like a physical manifestation of high-tier magic—a cosmic judge whose true face is not meant for mortal eyes.
* **Lore Context**: Assumed only when enacting devastating continent-level magic, mass-curses, or when dealing directly with Archangels. The Eclipse mask physically seals off her worldly senses, protecting her mortal mind from the vast, maddening influx of mana required for true magical omniscience.

---

## Tactical Profile (Magic Ranged)
The Sovereign of Guile is a supreme magic-ranged battlefield controller. Rather than engaging in melee, she alters the rules of engagement. Deploying her costs massive resources, but her basic attacks are devastating, targeted AoE blasts of dark matter. A detailed skill list including her buffs and ranged nukes can be found in her dedicated skills document.

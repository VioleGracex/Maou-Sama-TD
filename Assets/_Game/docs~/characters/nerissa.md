# Vassal: Nerissa
**Title**: Midnight Archivist  
**Rarity**: SR  
**Race**: Trueborn Demon  
**Class**: Sage  

## Visual Identity
- **Unique**: Floating runic books that circle her at all times.
- **Eyes**: Indigo glowing pupils.

## Tactical Profile
Strategic healer. Nerissa heals based on a percentage of the damage her allies have dealt in the last 2 seconds.

## Lore Fragment
The caretaker of the Citadel's Forbidden Library. She knows the true names of the Sanctus Empire's saints, which gives her a tactical edge against holy units.

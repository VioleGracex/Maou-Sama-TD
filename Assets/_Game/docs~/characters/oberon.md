# Vassal: Oberon
**Title**: Wildwood Lord  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Familiar (Fae)  
**Class**: Support  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Living garland, antler crown.
- **Unique**: Earthy, primordial aura.

## Tactical Profile
Oberon is a hybrid healer/buffer. His "Primal Sanctuary" creates a zone that constantly heals allies and increases their defense.

## Lore Fragment
An ancient spirit of the wild who predates the current nations of Aethelgard. They serve the Maou to ensure the balance of the world's mana is maintained.

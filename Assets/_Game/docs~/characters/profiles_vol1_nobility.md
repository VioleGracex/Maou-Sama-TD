# Legion Profiles Vol 1: The Iron Wall & The Frontline

## [1-8] The Bastions (Tanks)

### 1. [SSR] General Ignis (The Crimson Bastion)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer. Former Saint Knight.
*   **Appearance**: Long scarlet hair in a high ponytail, amber eyes. Wears heavy white-and-red plate armor.
*   **Demon Logic**: Grows obsidian "Crown Horns" in battle. Her armor leaks molten mana.
*   **Appeal**: Stoic, tall, "Knight-Commander" aura.

### 2. [SSR] Cogsworth (The Iron Butler)
*   **Origin**: Cursed Automaton.
*   **Appearance**: Porcelain skin, dapper tuxedo, clockwork ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: A furnace heart that glows orange when he enters "Overdrive Mode."
*   **Appeal**: Refined, dapper, "Mechanical Perfection."

### 3. [SSR] Balthazar (The Lord of Sloth)
*   **Origin**: True Demon Nobility.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid with massive curled ram horns and a perpetually sleepy expression.
*   **Demon Logic**: His horns grow to twice their size to block paths, emitting a "Lazy Aura" that slows enemies.
*   **Appeal**: Handsome, relaxed, high-fashion silk robes.

### 4. [SR] Golgotha (The Spiked Sentinel)
*   **Origin**: Undead Knight.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid frame in jagged black armor with glowing red eyes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Spikes burst from his armor when hit, dealing thorn damage.
*   **Appeal**: Intimidating, dark souls-esque aesthetic.

### 5. [SSR] Ursus (The Great Bear)
*   **Origin**: Bear Shifter.
*   **Appearance**: Muscular man with small furry ears and a tribal vest.
*   **Demon Logic**: In battle, he grows massive fur-covered arms and claws.
*   **Appeal**: Rugged, "Big Brother" energy, powerful build.

### 6. [R] Valerius (The Defector Sentry)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Simple human guard in dark empire armor with a "traitor's brand" on his cheek.
*   **Demon Logic**: Uses forbidden dark-mana stimulants to ignore pain.
*   **Appeal**: Relatable, tactical, "Gritty Mercenary" look.

### 7. [R] Onyx (The Living Stone)
*   **Origin**: Gargoyle Shifter.
*   **Appearance**: Grey marble skin, pointed ears, wings tucked as a cape.
*   **Demon Logic**: Turns to literal stone to block damage.
*   **Appeal**: Gothic, sculptural, quiet protector.

### 8. [SR] Hilda (The Fortress)
*   **Origin**: Demon Warrior.
*   **Appearance**: Tall woman with black horns and a shield the size of a door.
*   **Demon Logic**: Her shield is alive and has an eye that tracks intruders.
*   **Appeal**: Strong woman, "Valkyrie" vibes.

## [9-16] The Vanguards (Warriors)

### 9. [SSR] Drake (The Inferno Scale)
*   **Origin**: Dragonoid.
*   **Appearance**: Spiky orange hair, reptile eyes, small red horns.
*   **Demon Logic**: Dragon wings unfurl in battle; breath turns to magma.
*   **Appeal**: Hot-headed, athletic, "Shonen Protagonist" energy.

### 10. [SSR] Fenrir (The Moon-Eater)
*   **Origin**: Wolf Shifter.
*   **Appearance**: Grey wolf ears, rugged hair, handsome scarred face.
*   **Demon Logic**: Becomes a bipedal wolf beast in high-intensity combat.
*   **Appeal**: Wild, loyal, "Alpha" warrior.

### 11. [SSR] Arthas (The Oathbreaker)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Platinum hair, black armor, a "Cracked Black Halo."
*   **Demon Logic**: Wields a sword that drinks the light from the air.
*   **Appeal**: Fallen hero, tragic, "Edgy Lord" aesthetic.

### 12. [SR] Thorne (The Mercenary King)
*   **Origin**: Half-Demon.
*   **Appearance**: Single large horn, wild messy hair, long pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: His blood hardens into obsidian blades on his skin.
*   **Appeal**: Rugged, "Bad Boy" fighter.

### 13. [SSR] Leo (The Sun Lion)
*   **Origin**: Lion Shifter.
*   **Appearance**: Golden mane-like hair, pointed ears, sharp fangs.
*   **Demon Logic**: Radiant fire aura that burns nearby enemies.
*   **Appeal**: Regal, powerful, "Lion King" nobility.

### 14. [R] Kroc (The Swamp Sentry)
*   **Origin**: Lizard-kin.
*   **Appearance**: Scaled skin, yellow slit eyes, pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Can blend into the ground for ambush attacks.
*   **Appeal**: Exotic, primal, tactical.

### 15. [R] Boreas (The Frost Wolf)
*   **Origin**: Wolf-kin.
*   **Appearance**: Silver hair, long ears, pale blue eyes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Leaves a trail of frost that slows pursuers.
*   **Appeal**: Sleek, cold, "Ice Prince" vibe.

### 16. [SR] Gore (The Minotaur)
*   **Origin**: Demon Beast.
*   **Appearance**: Massive humanoid with bull horns and a nose ring.
*   **Demon Logic**: Berserker rage that increases ATK as HP drops.
*   **Appeal**: Brutish, heavy, powerful warrior.

## [17-20] The Executioners (Part 1)

### 17. [R] Whisper (The Silent Ghost)
*   **Origin**: Spectre.
*   **Appearance**: Translucent girl with blue fire hair and long spectral ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Passively ignores 50% of physical damage.
*   **Appeal**: Ethereal, melancholy, "Ghost Girl" aesthetic.

### 18. [SR] Kage (The Shadow Blade)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Ninja with glowing green eyes and dark mana tattoos.
*   **Demon Logic**: Can teleport between shadows.
*   **Appeal**: Tactical, mysterious, "Modern Ninja."

### 19. [SSR] Thanatos (The Grim Reaper)
*   **Origin**: Demon Spirit.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid in tattered silks with obsidian horns.
*   **Demon Logic**: Instantly kills enemies below 15% HP.
*   **Appeal**: Elegant, deadly, "Angel of Death" vibe.

### 20. [SSR] Azrael (The Angel of Death)
*   **Origin**: Fallen Angel.
*   **Appearance**: Six black wings, pointed ears, silver hair.
*   **Demon Logic**: Wings sharpen into blades for AoE attacks.
*   **Appeal**: Divine, tattered, "Beautiful Tragedy."

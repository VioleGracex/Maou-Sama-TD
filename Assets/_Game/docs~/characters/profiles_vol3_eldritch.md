# Legion Profiles Vol 3: The Divine Healers & The Dark Crafters

## [41-48] The Sages (Healers)

### 41. [SR] Vora (The Blood Nurse)
*   **Origin**: Vampire.
*   **Appearance**: Silver hair in a bun, crimson eyes, nurse-themed gothic dress.
*   **Demon Logic**: Heals allies by stealing life from enemies.
*   **Appeal**: Strict, clinical, "Gothic Nurse."

### 42. [R] Ivy (The Forest Spirit)
*   **Origin**: Dryad.
*   **Appearance**: Wooden skin, leaf-ears, green vines for hair.
*   **Demon Logic**: Passively regens HP for all allies on ground.
*   **Appeal**: Natural, gentle, "Nature Goddess."

### 43. [SSR] Lucia (The Weeping Angel)
*   **Origin**: Angel Traitor.
*   **Appearance**: Broken white wings, eyes that cry golden liquid.
*   **Demon Logic**: Resurrection skill (once per battle).
*   **Appeal**: Divine, sorrowful, "Fallen Saint."

### 44. [SSR] Melodia (The Siren)
*   **Origin**: Deep Sea Demon.
*   **Appearance**: Bioluminescent hair, scale jewelry, pointed ears.
*   **Demon Logic**: Songs increase the ASPD of all allies.
*   **Appeal**: Exotic, singing beauty.

### 45. [SR] Elara (The Traitor Saint)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Cracked black halo, dark neck-tattoos, silver robes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Turns incoming damage into healing for 5s.
*   **Appeal**: Corrupted purity, melancholy.

### 46. [SR] Coral (The Sea Sage)
*   **Origin**: Mermaid.
*   **Appearance**: Gills, blue skin, starfish hair clips.
*   **Demon Logic**: Cleanses all debuffs from allies.
*   **Appeal**: Playful, aquatic, colorful.

### 47. [SR] Sera (The Crimson Disciple)
*   **Origin**: Monk Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Red eyes, pointed ears, tactical monk robes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Deals damage while healing nearby allies.
*   **Appeal**: Disciplined, athletic, "Warrior Priestess."

### 48. [R] Mercy (The Merciful Sinner)
*   **Origin**: Fallen Nun.
*   **Appearance**: Small white horns, dark nun-robes, shy eyes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Sacrifices her own HP to fully heal an ally.
*   **Appeal**: Moe, "Innocent Corrupted."

## [49-54] The Architects (Towers/Traps)

### 49. [SR] Maiden (The Iron Kiss)
*   **Origin**: Animated Trap.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid inside an iron maiden shell.
*   **Demon Logic**: Snaps shut on enemies, dealing "Bleed."
*   **Appeal**: Macabre, industrial, "Iron Maiden" aesthetic.

### 50. [R] Watcher (The Stone Gargoyle)
*   **Origin**: Statue Spirit.
*   **Appearance**: Stone skin, glowing eyes, gargoyle wings.
*   **Demon Logic**: Long-range acid spit tower.
*   **Appeal**: Gothic, statuesque.

### 51. [SSR] Volt (The Storm Coil)
*   **Origin**: Cursed Machine.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid made of brass and electricity.
*   **Demon Logic**: Multi-target chain lightning tower.
*   **Appeal**: "Tesla" aesthetic, high-energy.

### 52. [SR] Piercer (The Heavy Ballista)
*   **Origin**: Magitech Cannon.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid fused with a giant crossbow.
*   **Demon Logic**: Infinite pierce (hits all in a line).
*   **Appeal**: Heavy artillery, tactical.

### 53. [SR] Glitch (The Reality Error)
*   **Origin**: Digital Ghost.
*   **Appearance**: Flickering humanoid, binary code aura.
*   **Demon Logic**: Stuns all enemies in a small area.
*   **Appeal**: Modern, technical, "Digital Horror."

### 54. [SSR] Incinerator (The Burner)
*   **Origin**: Dragon-Head Tower.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid in dragon armor holding a flamethrower.
*   **Demon Logic**: Continuous fire damage in a cone.
*   **Appeal**: Draconic, powerful, fiery.

## [55-60] The Necromancers (Summoners)

### 55. [R] Gloop (The Blue Slime)
*   **Origin**: Familiar.
*   **Appearance**: Cute blue slime with a tiny crown.
*   **Demon Logic**: Splits into two smaller slimes on death.
*   **Appeal**: Mascot, cute.

### 56. [R] Sparky (The Fire Imp)
*   **Origin**: Familiar.
*   **Appearance**: Small imp with goat horns and fire hair.
*   **Demon Logic**: Explodes on death.
*   **Appeal**: Mischievous, small demon.

### 57. [SSR] Marionette (The Doll Master)
*   **Origin**: Human Betrayer.
*   **Appearance**: Porcelain doll skin, pointed ears, velvet dress.
*   **Demon Logic**: Summons 3 porcelain puppet warriors.
*   **Appeal**: Victorian, creepy, "Master of Puppets."

### 58. [SSR] Adam (The Perfect Vessel)
*   **Origin**: Artificial Human.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid with glowing circuit-veins and no face.
*   **Demon Logic**: A high-HP tank summon.
*   **Appeal**: Sleek, technical, "The Ultimate Being."

### 59. [SR] Kel (The Arch Lich)
*   **Origin**: Ancient Undead.
*   **Appearance**: Skeleton in regal robes, glowing red eyes.
*   **Demon Logic**: Raises 5 skeletons from the ground.
*   **Appeal**: Overlord-style, classic necromancy.

### 60. [SR] Echo (The Sound Wraith)
*   **Origin**: Banshee.
*   **Appearance**: Humanoid with no mouth, communicating via mental pulses.
*   **Demon Logic**: Summons sound-clones that deal sonic damage.
*   **Appeal**: Melancholy, strange, spectral.

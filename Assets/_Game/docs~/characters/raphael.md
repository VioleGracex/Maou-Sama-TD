# Vassal: Raphael
**Title**: Golden Exile  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: The Fallen  
**Class**: Support  

## Visual Identity
- **Wings**: Tattered gold-white wings
- **Aura**: Radiates a soft, rebellious light.

## Tactical Profile
Raphael is a global buffer. While deployed, all allies generate SP 10% faster. His skill increases the ATK of all units currently on the field by 30% for 12 seconds.

## Lore Fragment
A high-ranking angel who was cast out for questioning the Luminary's "Final Purge" plan. He now finds the chaos of the demon realm strangely peaceful.

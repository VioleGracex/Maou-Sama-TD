# Vassal: Thorne
**Title**: The Mercenary King  
**Rarity**: SR  
**Race**: Mythological Hybrid (Demon)  
**Class**: Vanguard  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Messy hair, rugged features.
- **Unique**: Obsidian blades manifesting from his skin.

## Tactical Profile
Thorne is a DPS vanguard who thrives in the thick of combat. His obsidian blades provide a slight damage reflect chance, hurting anyone who strikes him in melee.

## Lore Fragment
A half-demon who grew up in the lawless Free Cities. He sold his sword to the highest bidder until the Maou's return offered a cause worth more than gold.

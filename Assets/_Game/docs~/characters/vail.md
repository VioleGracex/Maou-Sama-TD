# Vassal: Vail
**Title**: The Twilight Bard  
**Rarity**: SR  
**Race**: Human Betrayer  
**Class**: Support  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Shadow-dyed cloak, silver thread hat.
- **Weapon**: Whispering lyre.

## Tactical Profile
Vail is a mobile buffer. His "Rallying Requiem" buffs the attack speed of all allies in range, making him perfect for supporting a cluster of Rangers.

## Lore Fragment
A human composer who was banished for writing songs that humanized demons. He now writes the victory anthems of the Legion.

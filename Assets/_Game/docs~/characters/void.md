# Vassal: Void
**Title**: The Star-Eater  
**Rarity**: SSR  
**Race**: Spirit (Cosmic)  
**Class**: Warlock  

## Visual Identity
- **Features**: Silhouette filled with moving nebula stars.
- **Unique**: No true physical form.

## Tactical Profile
Void deals True Damage that ignores all enemy resistances. Her ultimate "Event Horizon" pulls all enemies in a large area towards the center of a black hole, dealing massive damage.

## Lore Fragment
A cosmic anomaly that drifted through "The Bleed" and was anchored to reality by the Maou's awakening ritual.

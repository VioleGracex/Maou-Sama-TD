# Dialogue Scripts

## Format Guide
Dialogues are stored in `JSON` format in `game/data.ts` (eventually moved to separate files).

## Script: Prologue - "The Awakening"
**Trigger**: Start of New Game (after Character Creation).

**Characters**:
*   Narrator
*   Lilith (Succubus)
*   Ignis (General)
*   Player (Maou)

**Flow**:
1.  **Narrator**: "Darkness. Absolute silence. Then... a heartbeat."
2.  **Lilith**: "It is time. Wake up, sleepyhead~"
3.  **Ignis**: "Show some respect, Witch! This is the resurrection of our Lord!"
4.  **Player (Choice)**:
    *   A: "Where... am I?"
    *   B: "Silence! My head hurts."
5.  **Ignis**:
    *   (If A): "You are in the Throne Room, Maou-sama. We successfully revived you."
    *   (If B): "F-forgive me! I will execute the noisy ones immediately!"
6.  **Lilith**: "Oh dear, Ignis is already hyperventilating. Look, Maou-sama, the humans are knocking on the front door. Shall we go say hello?"

## Script: 1-1 Post Battle
**Trigger**: Clear Stage 1-1.

**Ignis**: "The outer walls are secure. But their numbers... they are endless."
**Player**: "They are merely fodder. We need to strengthen our defenses."
**Lilith**: "I can summon more allies... for a price (Crystals)."

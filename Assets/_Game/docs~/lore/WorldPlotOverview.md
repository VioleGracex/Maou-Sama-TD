# Maou-Sama TD: World & Plot Overview

## Lore Context
The world is a dark fantasy realm where the **Maou-Sama** (Demon Lord) once ruled with absolute authority. Centuries ago, a "Holy Crusade" led by humanity and light-aligned spirits managed to seal the Demon Lord into a deep slumber, scattering their power and vassals across the continent.

The game begins at the moment of the **Great Awakening**. The seal has weakened, and the Demon Lord (the Player) is stirry. However, they are in a severely weakened state, confined to their inner sanctum.

## Core Characters

### The Demon Lord (Player)
- **Identity**: A powerful entity of shadow and authority.
- **State**: Recovering strength. Currently interacts through "Authority Seals" (UI/Deployment) and "Strategic Will."
- **Goal**: Reclaim the throne, gather lost vassals, and punish the interlopers.

### Hecatina (The Shadow Guide)
- **Identity**: A high-ranking succubus/wraith who remained loyal throughout the centuries.
- **Role**: Servant, tactical advisor, and the "voice" of the tutorial.
- **Personality**: Devoted, cold to enemies, slightly obsessive regarding the Demon Lord's comfort and power.

### Ignis (The First Vassal)
- **Identity**: A demonic knight of the Obsidian Flame.
- **Role**: The first defender recruited/awakened in the tutorial.
- **Combat Style**: Melee/Tank. Uses "Obsidian Aegis" to incinerate nearby foes.

## Plot Overview: Chapter 1 - The Ritual Breach

### Level 1: The Ritual Awakening
- **Location**: The Outer Corridor of the Shadow Sanctum.
- **The Conflict**: "Lesser Shadows" (echoes of the Demon Lord's lost power turned feral) and human scouts are breaching the sanctum entrance.
- **Key Event**: The Demon Lord awakens. Hecatina guides the placement of Ignis to hold the bottleneck. Ignis unleashes her ultimate to clear a massive cluster of shadows.

### The Path Forward
The Demon Lord must fight their way out of the sanctum, through the "Desolate Wastes" (the ruined empire), and eventually to the "Holy Capital" to reclaim the Heart of Darkness.

## Glossary for AI Writer
- **Authority Seals**: The energy used to manifest vassals in the world (Deployment Cost).
- **Vassals**: The collectible units/heroes serving the Demon Lord.
- **The Void Well**: The source of the Demon Lord's power, currently drained.
- **Sanctum**: The player's home base/dungeon.


# Factions & Nations of the Continent

While the "Demon Army" sees humans as a monolith, the human lands are divided into distinct nations with unique cultures, military doctrines, and motivations.

## 1. The Holy Empire of Sanctus (The Zealots)
*   **Location**: Central Plains (West of the Demon Border).
*   **Ideology**: Theocratic Absolutism. They worship "The Luminary". They believe Demons are a plague sent to test their faith.
*   **Leader**: **High Pontiff Valerius VI**. A shriveled old man kept alive by forbidden light magic.
*   **Military Doctrine**: "Holy Wall". Heavy usage of Paladins (Tanks) and Priests (Healers).
*   **Key Unit Types**:
    *   *Inquisitors*: Anti-magic units that silence Demon casters.
    *   *Crusaders*: High DEF units that buff nearby allies.
*   **Relationship to Maou**: The primary antagonist of Act 1-3. They initiated the Crusade.

## 2. The Ironclad Union (The Industrialists)
*   **Location**: The Smoky Mountains (South).
*   **Ideology**: Technocracy / Capitalism. They care little for religion but joined the war to test their weapons and secure mining rights for magical crystals.
*   **Leader**: **Prime Director Kaelen**. An inventor who replaced his own heart with a steam engine.
*   **Military Doctrine**: "Steel Rain". Ranged superiority.
*   **Key Unit Types**:
    *   *Musketeers*: Long-range physical damage.
    *   *Steam Golems*: Mechanical walkers with high HP but low magic resistance.
    *   *Sappers*: Enemies that place mines or destroy Demon traps.
*   **Relationship to Maou**: Opportunistic enemies. Can be negotiated with (via Shop/Trade mechanics later).

## 3. The Free Cities of Vane (The Mercenaries)
*   **Location**: The Coastal Archipelago (East).
*   **Ideology**: Libertarian / Pirate Code. A coalition of merchant princes and pirate lords.
*   **Leader**: **Lady Fortuna**. A mysterious woman who controls the sea trade routes.
*   **Military Doctrine**: "Guerilla Warfare". Speed and evasion.
*   **Key Unit Types**:
    *   *Duelists*: High dodge chance, ignores armor.
    *   *Bounty Hunters*: Target the highest value unit (The Maou/Castle) directly, ignoring blockers.
*   **Relationship to Maou**: Neutral/Hostile. They fight only if paid by Sanctus.

## 4. The Frostbound Clans (The Survivalists)
*   **Location**: The Glacial North.
*   **Ideology**: Survival of the Fittest. They respect strength above all else.
*   **Leader**: **Warchief Bjorn**. A half-giant.
*   **Military Doctrine**: "Berserker Barrage".
*   **Key Unit Types**:
    *   *Berserkers*: ATK increases as HP decreases.
    *   *Shamans*: Summon spirit wolves.
*   **Relationship to Maou**: Potential allies. They hate the Sanctus Empire for burning their forests. If the Maou proves their strength in Act 4, they may join the Demon Army.

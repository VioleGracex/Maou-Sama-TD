
# The Multiverse & The Interlopers

## 1. The Concept: "The Bleed"
The world of Aethelgard is rich in Mana. This density sometimes causes the fabric of reality to thin, creating rifts known as "The Bleed".
Historically, small items would fall through. However, the Holy Empire of Sanctus has developed a "Summoning Ritual" to force these rifts open, pulling sentient beings from other dimensions to fight their war.

## 2. The Otherworlds

### Terra Prime (Modern Earth)
*   **Description**: A world of zero magic but extreme technological advancement.
*   **The Summoned**: Usually teenagers or salarymen.
*   **Ability**: "Cheat Skills". Upon entering Aethelgard, they are granted broken abilities by the laws of physics clashing with magic.
*   **Unit Archetype**: The "Hero". High stats, unique UI-breaking skills (e.g., "Save Scumming" - rewinding time 5 seconds).

### Cyberia-09 (Cyberpunk Dystopia)
*   **Description**: A world of neon, chrome, and corporate warfare.
*   **The Summoned**: Cyborg soldiers, Hackers.
*   **Ability**: "Tech-Integration". They can merge with Aethelgard's Golems or ancient artifacts.
*   **Unit Archetype**: The "Gunner". extremely high fire rate, laser weaponry (Pure damage).

### Eldoria (High Fantasy)
*   **Description**: A world where magic is wild and untamed.
*   **The Summoned**: Elven Mages, Dragon Riders.
*   **Ability**: "Overload". Their magic is stronger but volatile.
*   **Unit Archetype**: "Glass Cannon". Massive AoE damage.

## 3. The "Isekai" Faction (The Interlopers)
Eventually, the summoned heroes realize they are being used. They band together to form a third faction, unrelated to Demons or Humans.
*   **Goal**: To find a way home, or to conquer this world using their superior knowledge/tech.
*   **Leader**: **Kenji (The Glitched Hero)**. An Earth teenager who realized the "Holy Empire" are the bad guys, but decided he would rather be the God of the new world than save it.

## 4. Gameplay Implications
*   **New Enemy Types**:
    *   *Truck-kun*: A boss unit that charges down a lane, instantly killing ground units.
    *   *The Protagonist*: Has "Plot Armor" (Invulnerability for first 10 seconds).
*   **New Gacha Pool**: "Otherworldly Banners". Allows the Maou to recruit defecting Isekai heroes.

# The Races of Aethelgard: Aesthetic & Manifestation

In Aethelgard, power is defined by its elegance. The Maou's Legion consists of various races, all of which adhere to the **"Apex Humanoid"** standard—visual forms that are pleasing, charismatic, and intensely expressive to the human eye. 

## The Dual-Form System
Every sentient being in the Legion exists in two primary states:

1.  **Social/Citadel Form**: The "Waifu/Husbando" state seen in the Home Screen, Royal Chambers, and Dialogue. These forms emphasize peak aesthetic appeal, fashion, and personality.
2.  **Combat/Manifest Form**: The tactical "Battle Sprite" state. During combat, units manifest their racial power more aggressively (manifesting wings, glowing runes, spectral limbs, or mechanical weaponry) to exert dominance on the grid.

---

## 1. Trueborn Demons (Abyssal Nobility)
The ruling elite of the Abyss. They embody authoritative beauty.
- **Aesthetic**: Elegant, noble, often wearing high-fashion silks and obsidian accessories.
- **Combat Manifest**: Massive mana-wings and crown-like glowing horns.
- **Example**: Lilith (Succubus), Balthazar (Lord of Sloth).

## 2. Shifters (Beast-kin Beauties)
Ancient tribes that share a bloodline with primordial beasts.
- **Aesthetic**: Energetic and wild with animal ears (Kemonomimi) and tails. They retain human-level attractiveness with subtle wild traits.
- **Combat Manifest**: Limb transformation (claws, fur) and bestial speed.
- **Example**: Fenrir (Wolf), Leo (Lion).

## 3. Imperial Defectors (Renegades)
Former heroes of the Holy Empire who found the Maou’s path more "alluring."
- **Aesthetic**: Disciplined, "cool/edgy" military fashion with glowing mana-tattoos.
- **Combat Manifest**: Summoned spectral weaponry and floating sigils.
- **Example**: General Ignis, Astrid, Selene.

## 4. The Fallen (Celestial Traitors)
Angels who rebelled against the cold perfection of the Light.
- **Aesthetic**: Ethereal, tattered silk, and "Broken Halo" accessories.
- **Combat Manifest**: Large, obsidian-tattered wings and rain of black feathers.
- **Example**: Azrael, Lucia.

## 5. Constructs (Perfected Gynoids & Automata)
Artificial lifeforms perfected by the Maou’s engineers.
- **Aesthetic**: Porcelain skin, clockwork accessories, and "Perfect Android" features. They never look like "clunky robots"—only masterpieces of engineering.
- **Combat Manifest**: Hidden cannons, steam-venting armor, and jet-thrusters.
- **Example**: Cogsworth.

## 6. Mythological Hybrids (Monster Beauties)
Exotic races with traits from ancient myths.
- **Aesthetic**: Exotic features like feathered ears (Harpies), snake-hair braids (Gorgons), or dragon-horns.
- **Combat Manifest**: Full wing span or elemental breath manifestations.
- **Example**: Aquila, Stheno.

---

## 7. The Fodder (Familiars)
Lower-tier entities that lack a human soul.
- **Aesthetic**: Adorable slimes or mischievous chibis. They serve as the Legion's mascots.
- **Example**: Gloop.

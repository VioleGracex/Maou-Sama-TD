# Maou-Sama TD: The World Bible (Master Edition)

## 1. The Core Premise: The Pact with Gaia
The world of **Aethelgard** is not a simple stage for Good vs. Evil. It is a living system (**Gaia**) that uses the cycle of Heroes and Demon Lords to maintain its own stability.

### The Stalemate & The Resurrection
Fifty years ago, the **13th Demon Lord (Player)** and the **Hero of Heroes** killed each other in a catastrophic stalemate. In their final moments, the Maou forced a **Sovereign Pact** with Gaia: "Resurrect us both for a rematch."

Gaia accepted, but for its own ends. The world is being invaded by the **Inverse World**—an eldritch dimension of deleted data and tattered reality created by the **First Maou**. Gaia needs its two strongest "antibodies" (Maou and Hero) to survive and unite against this existential threat.

---

## 2. Key Characters & Factions

### The Demon Lord (The Player)
The 13th in the line of Maous. Resurrected but bound by a Pact that prevents him from killing the True Hero until the "threat to the asset" is gone. He leads the **Maou’s Legion** from the **Shadow Sanctum**.

### Ignis (The Betrayed Vanguard)
*   **True Identity**: A former Human Hero from 50 years ago.
*   **The Betrayal**: After the Great War, the Holy Empire feared her and tried to assassinate her. The past Maou saved her life.
*   **Role**: The first General to rejoin the Player. She guarded the Maou's tomb for five decades.

### Hecatina (The Shadow Guide)
The high-ranking wraith/succubus who orchestrated the Great Awakening. She serves as the tactical voice and devoted servant of the Maou.

### The Rival (The True Hero)
The original Hero from the Pact, resurrected and fueled by rage. Originally an antagonist seeking a rematch, he becomes an "Unwilling Ally" in Act 3 when the Inverse World invades.

### The Interlopers (Otherworlders)
Entities summoned by the Holy Empire from other worlds (Terra Prime, Cyberia, etc.) using the "Summoning Ritual." They often defect or form their own factions.

---

## 3. The Nations of Aethelgard

### The Holy Empire of Sanctus
The primary human antagonist. A theocratic nation led by **High Pontiff Valerius VI**. They occupy the former demon lands and use the "Hero Clone" project to maintain control.

### The Trade Republic (Ironclad Union & Free Cities)
A collection of merchants and industrialists who care more for mana-mining and trade routes than holy war. They represent the "Greed" of humanity.

### The Frostbound Clans
Northern survivalists who respect strength. Potential allies for the Maou in the later acts.

---

## 4. The Path of the Story
*   **Act 1**: Reclaiming the Ruined Kingdom. Defeating the Adventurer Guilds and reuniting with **Ignis**, **Shade**, and **Lilith**.
*   **Act 2**: The Great Crusade. Marching on the Sanctus Capital and defeating the "False Hero" **Arthur** only to face the **True Rival** descend from the sky.
*   **Act 3**: The Inverse Invasion. The world "glitches." The Maou is forced into a Pact-bound alliance with the Hero to defeat the **First Maou** and save existence from being "deleted."
